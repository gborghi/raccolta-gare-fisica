# OII 2006 1° Livello — Quesito 39

|-----------|-----------|
| protone   | $1{,}0073$ |
| neutrone  | $1{,}0087$ |
| ${}^6_3\text{Li}$ | $6{,}0135$ |

$1\ \text{u} = 1{,}66 \times 10^{-27}\ \text{kg}$
