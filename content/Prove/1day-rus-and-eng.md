## Problema 1

Given a trapezoid $ABCD$ ($AD \parallel BC$) with $\angle ABC > 90°$. Point $M$ is chosen on the lateral side $AB$. Let $O_1$ and $O_2$ be the circumcenters of the triangles $MAD$ and $MBC$ respectively. The circumcircles of the triangles $MO_1D$ and $MO_2C$ meet again at the point $N$. Prove that the line $O_1O_2$ passes through the point $N$.

## Problema 2

Find all odd positive integers $n > 1$ such that there is a permutation $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$ of the numbers $1, 2, \ldots, n$, where for each $k$ with $1 \le k \le n$, $n$ divides one of the numbers $a_k^2 - a_{k+1} - 1$ and $a_k^2 - a_{k+1} + 1$ (we assume $a_{n+1} = a_1$).
