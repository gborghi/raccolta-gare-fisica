# OII 2014 1° Livello — Quesito 20

(A) $1.2 \times 10^{2}\ \text{N}$
(B) $1.2 \times 10^{3}\ \text{N}$
(C) $1.2 \times 10^{4}\ \text{N}$
(D) $1.2 \times 10^{5}\ \text{N}$
(E) $1.2 \times 10^{6}\ \text{N}$
