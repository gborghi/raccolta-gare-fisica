## Problema 10

An a.c. voltmeter displays the rms value of the voltage, $V_{rms}$, for a.c. signals and also for periodic signals that are not sinusoidal. Determine the following:

(i) the mean voltage, i.e. $\langle V \rangle$.

(ii) the rms voltage, i.e. $V_{rms}$.

(iii) the rms value of deviation from the mean, $\langle(V - \langle V \rangle)^2\rangle^{1/2}$, which is $\sigma_{rms}$.
