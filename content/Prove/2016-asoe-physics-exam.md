## Problema 3

Question 3

produces when they hit a golf ball off the tee. David derives the following formula for his slice:
$$\phi = \frac{(1-P)}{(H-L)\sin\theta}.$$

with H > L, and an angle $\theta$ (measured in degrees) that can range between $0^\circ$ and $180^\circ$.
Which of the following options will minimise David’s slice?
a. Make P as small as possible, keeping the other variables constant.
b. Make H as small as possible, keeping the other variables constant.
c. Make L as small as possible, keeping the other variables constant.
d. Make $\theta$ as small as possible, keeping the other variables constant.
e. None of the above will help David minimise his slice.
