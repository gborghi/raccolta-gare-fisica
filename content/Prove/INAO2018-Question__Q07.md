# INAO 2018 — Problema 7

Yash discovered a binary star, consisting of a very light star A in a circular orbit around a massive star B ($m_A \ll m_B$). He could measure the component of velocity of star A along the line of sight via Doppler shift. His measurements are tabulated below. All the data were obtained on 17th July 2009, and the times given are in UT.

(a) (10 marks) Plot the data, and measure the orbital period of star A from the plot.

(b) (5 marks) Use this information to calculate the mass of star B, in terms of solar mass.

(c) (3 marks) Is the plot symmetric with respect to time axis? If yes, what does it signify? If no, what is the reason?
