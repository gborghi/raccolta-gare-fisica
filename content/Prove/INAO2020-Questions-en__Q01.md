# INAO 2020 — Problema 1

(10 marks) On the evening of an autumnal equinox day Siddhant noticed that Mars was exactly along the north-south meridian in his sky at the exact moment when the sun was setting. In other words, the Sun and Mars subtended an angle of exactly $90^\circ$ as measured from the Earth. If the orbital radius of Mars is 1.52 au, what will be the approximate rise time of the Mars on the next autumnal equinox day?
