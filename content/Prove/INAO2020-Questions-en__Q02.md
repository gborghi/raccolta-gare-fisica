# INAO 2020 — Problema 2

Manoj was determined to claim some world record. He got an idea from the fairy tale of Rapunzel that he will never cut his hair and he can claim world record for growing longest hair.

(a) (3 marks) Estimate maximum length of the hair that he can grow in his whole life, if he hasn't cut his hair from his birth.

(b) (7 marks) What will be mass of these hair? (density of typical hair strand is $1.3\ \text{g/cm}^3$) If you make any simplifying assumptions, discuss qualitatively how answer would have been affected if those assumptions were not made.
