# INAO 2025 — Problema 7

A spacecraft is in a circular orbit around the Sun with an orbital radius of 1 A.U. The spacecraft deploys a panel of area $A$ with the normal of the panel pointing towards the Sun. The panel is made of a perfectly reflecting material. What can be the maximum mass $M$ of the spacecraft (including that of the panel) such that the spacecraft escapes the solar system? [5]
