# INJSO 2017 — Problema 15

A compound exists in the gaseous state both as monomer (A) and dimer (A$_2$). The molecular weight of the monomer is 48. In an experiment, 96 g of the compound was confined in a vessel of volume 33.6 litres and heated to 273°C. Calculate the pressure developed, if the compound exists as a dimer to the extent of 50% by weight under this condition, assuming that temperature does not affect the physical state of the dimer.

- (a) 0.67 atm
- (b) 1 atm
- (c) 1.33 atm
- (d) 2 atm
