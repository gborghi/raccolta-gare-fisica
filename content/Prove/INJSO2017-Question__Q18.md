# INJSO 2017 — Problema 18

Following are some statements about mitochondria and chloroplasts:

I. Mitochondrion has double stranded DNA that replicates independently while chloroplast does not have the same.
II. Both mitochondria and chloroplast have double stranded DNA that replicates independently.
III. Both mitochondria and chloroplast have single stranded DNA replicating independently.
IV. Mitochondria and chloroplast have both RNA and ribosomes.

Which of the above statements are correct?

- (a) Both I and IV
- (b) Both III and IV
- (c) Both II and III
- (d) Both II and IV
