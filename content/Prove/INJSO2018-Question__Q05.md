# INJSO 2018 — Problema 5

A particle moves along positive X-axis with a velocity (in a suitable unit) varying as $(4 - 3t - t^2)$ where $t$ is time in seconds. Students draw following four conclusions.

(I) Initially the particle is at the origin.
(II) Initial velocity of the particle is positive.
(III) The particle decelerates till $t = 1$ s.
(IV) The speed of the particle goes on increasing after $t = 1$ s.

Therefore, conclusions

- (a) (I) & (II) are sometimes correct, (III) & (IV) are always correct.
- (b) (I), (II) & (IV) are always correct, (III) is sometime correct.
- (c) (I), (III) & (IV) are always correct, (II) is never correct.
- (d) (I) is sometimes correct, (II) is never correct, (III) & (IV) are always correct.
