# INJSO 2018 — Problema 11

A beaker of mass 50 g, with 262 g of water in it, is kept on a weighing machine. A uniform hollow ball of total volume 36 cc is inserted into the water in the beaker, so that the ball floats on water. The weighing machine now reads 400 g. Density of brass is 8 g/cc, while that of the air inside the brass ball can be neglected. Thickness of the brass used to prepare the ball must be nearest to ______.

- (a) 0.5 mm
- (b) 0.8 mm
- (c) 1 mm
- (d) 1.2 mm
