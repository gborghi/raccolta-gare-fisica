# INJSO 2018 — Problema 19

At time $t = 0$, an object is dropped from point A that rebounds (losing some of its kinetic energy) with a speed of 10 m/s after making a collision with the ground. It finally attains maximum possible height at $t = 4$ s. What is the difference of heights at $t = 0$ and at $t = 4$ s? (Ignore air resistance as well as the time of impact)

- (a) 35 m
- (b) 40 m
- (c) 45 m
- (d) zero
