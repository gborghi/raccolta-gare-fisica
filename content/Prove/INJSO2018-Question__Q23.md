# INJSO 2018 — Problema 23

Cone cells in human eye are responsible for color vision. The cone cells are of three types: Red (R), Green (G) and Blue (B) cells. Each type of cone cell responds to a range of wavelengths, with a particular wavelength showing the maximum response. Given below is a graph showing relative response of rods and cone cells in different wavelengths of light.

Which of the cone cell(s) will respond to wavelengths in the orange region?

- (a) Blue and red
- (b) Red and green
- (c) Blue and green
- (d) only red
