# INJSO 2018 — Problema 24

Five polyester balls labelled P, Q, R, S & T are suspended from insulating threads. Several experiments are performed on the balls and the following observations are made.

I. Ball P repels R and attracts Q.
II. Ball S attracts Q and has no effect on T.
III. A negatively charged rod attracts both P and T.

Which one of the following options correctly describes the nature of charges on the respective balls (0 refers to uncharged)?

|   | P | Q | R | S | T |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| a | + | − | + | 0 | + |
| b | + | − | + | + | 0 |
| c | − | + | − | 0 | 0 |
| d | + | − | + | 0 | 0 |
