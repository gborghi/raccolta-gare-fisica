# INJSO 2018 — Problema 25

Plant tissues recover carbon dioxide that occurs as 'source'. The product(s) is/are then transported to different parts of the plant body for its further utilization or storage, which is known as 'sink'. Which of the following can be categorized as 'source (P)' and 'Sink (Q)'?

- (a) (P) endodermis of root; (Q) phloem cells of root
- (b) (P) epidermal region of assimilatory root; (Q) meristematic region of assimilatory root
- (c) (P) parenchyma of beet tuber; (Q) phloem cells of root
- (d) (P) xylem tissue of a developing leaf; (Q) phloem tissue of the adjacent green leaf
