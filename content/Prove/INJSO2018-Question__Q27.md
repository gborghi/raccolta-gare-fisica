# INJSO 2018 — Problema 27

A student titrated a mixture of NaHCO$_3$ and Na$_2$CO$_3$ against a standard solution of HCl. He pipetted out a certain volume of mixture and added phenolphthalein indicator. The pink solution changed to colourless after addition of $x$ mL of HCl. To the same solution he added methyl orange indicator and continued the titration. The end point with methyl orange indicator was obtained after addition of $y$ mL HCl. The volume of HCl required for complete neutralization of Na$_2$CO$_3$ is ______.

- (a) 2x
- (b) x/2
- (c) y
- (d) y − x
