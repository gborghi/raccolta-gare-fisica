# INJSO 2018 — Problema 33

A sprint runner was running a 100 meter race. 10 min before starting his run in the race, he was given 200 ml of 5% glucose solution. (Assume M.W. of glucose = 180.0). [Total=5 marks]

I) Calculate how many moles of glucose was given to the sprinter. [1.5]

II) If 100% of the glucose molecules was absorbed into his body and were used for production of pyruvic acid, calculate the moles of pyruvic acid that will be produced from these glucose molecules. [0.5]

III) 25% of the total pyruvic acids generated from these glucose molecules are in muscle tissues and they subsequently form lactic acid. Calculate the total moles of lactic acid produced from these pyruvic acids. [0.5]

IV) If all the pyruvic acid generated (including the muscles and other parts of body) undergo aerobic oxidation to produce CO$_2$ and water, calculate the total moles of CO$_2$ produced from these pyruvic acid. [1.0]

V) The figure below represents a pictorial representation of components of a cell. Based on the information above predict the sites where these products are formed when glucose is metabolized by aerobic or anaerobic oxidation. [1.5]
i. The site of formation of pyruvic acid is ____________ in the cell.
ii. The site of lactic acid formation is ____________ in the cell.
iii. The site of oxidation of pyruvic acid for generation of end products such as CO$_2$ and H$_2$O is ____________.
