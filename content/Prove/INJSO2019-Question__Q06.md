|---|---|
| I | 0.68 |
| II | 0.21 |
| III | 1.65 |
| IV | 0.07 |

Animals I – IV most likely could be respectively:

- (A) Elephant, Cat, Human and Mouse
- (B) Cat, Mouse, Elephant and Human
- (C) Human, Cat, Elephant and Mouse
- (D) Cat, Human, Mouse and Elephant
