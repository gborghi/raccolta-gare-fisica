# INJSO 2019 — Problema 13

An LPG gas cylinder regularly used in the household contains a mixture of butane and propane. If 5 litres of this mixture on complete combustion produces 17 litres of $\text{CO}_2$ at atmospheric pressure and 25 °C, then the ratio of butane to propane in the mixture is (Assume that both the gases in the cylinder are in vapour phase.)

- (A) 3:2
- (B) 2:3
- (C) 4:1
- (D) 1:4
