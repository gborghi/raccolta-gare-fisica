# INJSO 2019 — Problema 14

In a chemistry laboratory, a student found a bottle labeled 'Acid'. As it was a solid, she was curious to find out what it is. She weighed 0.42 g of this and made a solution of it and titrated it with 0.17 M NaOH solution. The volume of NaOH required to obtain the end point was 33.8 mL. If the molecular formula of the acid is $\text{C}_6\text{H}_{10}\text{O}_4$, find out the number of protons per acid molecule that take part in the reaction and the amount of acid required to neutralize 1 mole of the alkali.

- (A) 1 proton and 73 g
- (B) 2 protons and 146 g
- (C) 1 proton and 46 g
- (D) 2 protons and 73 g
