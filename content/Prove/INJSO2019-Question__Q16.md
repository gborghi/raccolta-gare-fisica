# INJSO 2019 — Problema 16

The position of some metals in the electrochemical series in decreasing electropositive character is $\text{Mg} > \text{Al} > \text{Zn} > \text{Cu} > \text{Ag}$. In a chemical factory, a worker by accident used a copper rod to stir a solution of aluminum nitrate; he was scared that now there would be some reaction in the solution, so he hurriedly removed the rod from the solution and observed that

- (A) the rod was coated with Al.
- (B) an alloy of Cu and Al was being formed.
- (C) the solution turned blue in colour.
- (D) there was no reaction.
