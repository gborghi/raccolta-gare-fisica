# INJSO 2019 — Problema 21

A particle is travelling with uniform acceleration of magnitude $a$. During successive time intervals $t_1$, $t_2$ and $t_3$ its average velocities are $v_1$, $v_2$ and $v_3$ respectively. Then

- (A) $\dfrac{v_2 - v_1}{t_2 + t_1} = \dfrac{v_3 - v_2}{t_3 + t_2}$
- (B) $\dfrac{v_2 - v_1}{t_2 - t_1} = \dfrac{v_3 - v_2}{t_3 - t_2}$
- (C) $\dfrac{v_2 + v_1}{t_2 + t_1} = \dfrac{v_3 + v_2}{t_3 + t_2}$
- (D) $\dfrac{v_2 - v_1}{t_2 + t_1} \neq \dfrac{v_3 - v_2}{t_3 + t_2}$
