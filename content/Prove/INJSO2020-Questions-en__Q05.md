# INJSO 2020 — Problema 5

A current carrying wire is bent in the shape shown below. Direction of current is also shown in the figure. The wire forms a three-dimensional cubical shape of side $2b$ with a current $I$ flowing through it, with point $P$ at the centre. The direction of magnetic field at the center $P$ of the cubical shape will be

- (A) parallel to the $x$ axis.
- (B) parallel to the $y$ axis.
- (C) parallel to the $z$ axis.
- (D) undefined (field will be zero).
