# INJSO 2020 — Problema 17

A 5 cm long needle is placed along the principal axis of a concave mirror of a focal length 10 cm. It is observed that one end of the image of the needle coincides with one of the ends of the needle. The other end of the image is at a distance $x$ from the pole of the mirror, where $x$ is

- (A) $20\,\text{cm}$
- (B) $\dfrac{50}{3}\,\text{cm}$
- (C) $30\,\text{cm}$
- (D) $10\,\text{cm}$
