# INJSO 2020 — Problema 25

**Section III (analytical)**

[6 marks] Consider a toy model of E. coli cell (bacterial cell) as a cylindrical body with hemispherical caps at both ends of the cylinder. The diameter of this cylinder is taken as $1\,\mu\text{m}$ and the length of its cylindrical part is also $1\,\mu\text{m}$.

(a) Estimate the average distance between two E. coli cells (centre to centre distance) in a saturated growth medium having saturation density of E. coli cells of about $10^9\,\text{cells/mL}$.

(b) Many biochemical studies specify the concentration of proteins in a cell in units of nanomolar (nM) concentration. If such a protein species inside an E. coli cell has a concentration of at least 20 nM, how many minimum molecules of that protein species are present in each cell?
