# INJSO 2023 — Problema 5

Movement of ions in and out of guard cells in plants is responsible for the stomatal activity (i.e. opening and closing of stomata). An experiment was carried out on two broad bean plants (Plant I and II) using a radioactive isotope of potassium. The concentration of potassium ions in the two guard cells of each plant was measured using a radioactivity counter. The graph below shows the K$^+$ concentration (indicated as potassium X-ray counts per sec) in each of the guard cells in respective plants, I and II.

Based on the results, which of the following is true?

- (a) I has open stomata most likely for transpiration.
- (b) II has open stomata most likely due to exposure to light.
- (c) II has open stomata most likely due to absence of light.
- (d) I has open stomata for uptake of carbon dioxide.
