# INJSO 2023 — Problema 7

Wood pulp contains multiple compounds, including several polymers. Hydrolysis of one of the polymers produces compound $\alpha$. This compound $\alpha$ undergoes anaerobic decomposition by microbes and produces compounds $\beta$ and $\gamma$.

Compounds $\alpha$, $\beta$ and $\gamma$, respectively, are:

- (a) Cellulose, ethanol, water
- (b) Glucose, ethanol, carbon dioxide
- (c) Lactose, lactic acid, carbon dioxide
- (d) Starch, ethanoic acid, carbon dioxide
