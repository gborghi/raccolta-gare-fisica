# INJSO 2023 — Problema 8

Read the statements given below:

(i) Dissolution of glucose in water is an exothermic process.
(ii) Mixing of calcium oxide in water is an endothermic process.
(iii) Melting of ice into water is an endothermic process.
(iv) Dilution of sulphuric acid in water is an endothermic process.
(v) Boiling of water is an exothermic process.

Of the above, the true statement/s is/are:

- (a) (iii)
- (b) (v) and (i)
- (c) (iv) and (v)
- (d) (i), (ii), and (iv)
