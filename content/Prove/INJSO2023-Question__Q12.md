# INJSO 2023 — Problema 12

Two identical iron balls of mass 10 g each are moving in space at speeds 10 m/s and 5 m/s along the same direction with the faster one following the slower one. The balls collide, stick together, and continue to move as a single object. The loss of kinetic energy during collision increases the temperature of the combined object. Rise in temperature of the combined object is roughly ...

**Note:**
- Specific heat capacity of iron is 451 J/(kg K).
- Neglect any other process that may change the temperature.
- Initial temperature of both the balls is assumed to be the same.

- (a) 0.007 K
- (b) 0.014 K
- (c) 0.07 K
- (d) 0.14 K
