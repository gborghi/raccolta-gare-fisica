# INJSO 2023 — Problema 21

A member of alkene series X has a molecular mass 28 amu. A small quantity of X (150 cm$^3$) is burnt in just sufficient air (containing 20% oxygen) to form carbon dioxide and steam. If all the measurements are made at 1 atm pressure and 100 $^\circ$C, then the composition of the products formed and the unreacted air is ...

- (a) 300 cm$^3$ CO$_2$, 300 cm$^3$ steam, and 450 cm$^3$ the unreacted air, respectively
- (b) $5.9 \times 10^{21}$ molecules of CO$_2$, $5.9 \times 10^{21}$ molecules of steam, 1800 cm$^3$ the unreacted air, respectively
- (c) $5.9 \times 10^{25}$ molecules of CO$_2$, $5.9 \times 10^{25}$ molecules of steam, 450 cm$^3$ the unreacted air, respectively
- (d) 300 cm$^3$ CO$_2$, 300 cm$^3$ steam, and 1800 cm$^3$ the unreacted air, respectively
