# INJSO 2023 — Problema 24

Prajakta is riding her cycle on a level road. She applies brake and the cycle slows down. Select the correct statement/s.

- (a) If she applies only the front brake, the force due to both the tyres reduce her cycle's speed.
- (b) If she applies only the front brake, the force due to ground reduces her cycle's speed.
- (c) If she applies only the rear brake, the force due to the rear tyre reduces her cycle's speed.
- (d) If she applies only the rear brake, the force due to ground reduces her cycle's speed.
