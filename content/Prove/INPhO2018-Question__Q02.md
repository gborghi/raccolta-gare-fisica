# INPhO 2018 — Problema 2

An opaque sphere of radius $R$ lies on a horizontal plane. On the perpendicular through the point of contact, there is a point source of light at a distance $R$ above the top of the sphere (i.e. $3R$ from the plane). **[Marks: 8]**

(a) Find the area of the shadow of the sphere on the plane. **[2]**

(b) A transparent liquid of refractive index $\sqrt{3}$ is filled above the plane such that the sphere is just covered with liquid. Find the area of the shadow of the sphere on the plane now. **[6]**
