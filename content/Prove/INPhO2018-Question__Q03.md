# INPhO 2018 — Problema 3

Consider an infinite ladder of resistors. The input current $I_0$ is indicated in the figure. **[Marks: 12]**

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 3**
![[INPhO2018-Question_p2_f1.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

(a) Find the equivalent resistance of the ladder. **[2]**

(b) Find the recursion relation obeyed by the currents through the horizontal resistors $r$. You will get a relationship where $I_n$ will be related to (may be several) $I_i$s, $i < n$, $n > 0$. **[2]**

(c) Solve this for the special case $R = r$ to obtain $I_n$ and $I'_n$ as explicit functions of $n$. You may have to make a reasonable assumption about the behaviour of $I_n$ as $n$ becomes large. **[4]**

(d) If the ladder is chopped off after the $N$-th node (so that $I_{N+1} = 0$) what will the form of $\dfrac{I_n}{I_N}$ be for $n \leq N$? **[4]**
