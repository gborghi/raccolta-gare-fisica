# INPhO 2018 — Problema 4

An hour glass is placed on a weighing scale. Initially all the sand of mass $m_0$ kg in the glass is held in the upper reservoir (ABC) and the mass of the glass alone is $M$ kg. At $t = 0$, the sand is released. It exits the upper reservoir at constant rate $\dfrac{dm}{dt} = \lambda$ kg/s where $m$ is the mass of the sand in the upper reservoir at time $t$ sec. Assume that the speed of the falling sand is zero at the neck of the glass and after it falls through a constant height $h$ it instantaneously comes to rest on the floor (DE) of the hour glass. Obtain the reading on the scale for all times $t > 0$. Make a detailed plot of the reading vs time. **[Marks: 9]**
