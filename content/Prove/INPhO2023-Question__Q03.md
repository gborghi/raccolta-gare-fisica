# INPhO 2023 — Problema 3

**3. [16 marks] Work in progress**

One mole of an ideal monoatomic gas goes through a linear process from A to B as shown in the pressure-volume ($P$-$V$) diagram. The temperature at A is $T_A = 227\ ^\circ\text{C}$. The process is such that, the temperature decreases and the heat is continuously supplied to the gas. The ratio of the specific heat at the constant pressure to that at the constant volume is $5/3$. Obtain the expression for the maximum work ($W_\text{max}$) the gas can perform in such a process. Calculate $W_\text{max}$.
