# IOQA 2022 (Part II) — Problema 3

**Spherical aberration**

A spherical concave mirror gives a real image of a distant object with some spherical aberration. Consider a concave spherical mirror with radius of curvature $R$. The radius of its rim is $r$.

(a) **[12]** For monochromatic light rays coming from infinity and parallel to the optic axis, calculate the size of the image ($I_s$) at the focal plane (taken to be at $R/2$ distance away from the centre of the mirror) if $R = 1500$ mm and $r = 75$ mm.

(b) **[3]** What limit does this spherical aberration place on the angular resolution ($\theta_{min}$)?
