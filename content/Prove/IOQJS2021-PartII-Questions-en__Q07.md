# IOQJS 2021 (Part II) — Problema 7

Consider the following two statements:

Statement S1: If you put 100 g ice at 0°C and 100 g water at 0°C into a freezer, which is maintained at −10°C, the ice will eventually lose the larger amount of heat.

Statement S2: At 0°C, water is denser than ice.

Choose the correct statement among the following.

- (A) Both S1 and S2 are true and S2 is the correct explanation of S1.
- (B) Both S1 and S2 are true but S2 is not the correct explanation of S1.
- (C) S1 is true but S2 is false.
- (D) S1 is false but S2 is true.
