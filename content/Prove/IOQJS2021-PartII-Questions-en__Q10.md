# IOQJS 2021 (Part II) — Problema 10

Jyoti was asked by her mother to add a pinch of potassium permanganate to water in a container to disinfect it. As she added the crystals and observed the changes in water, the phenomena of diffusion came to her mind. She wrote the following statements. Identify the statement which Jyoti wrote that is **incorrect**.

- (A) When the entire liquid is of uniform color, no further diffusion can be observed.
- (B) The diffusion gets completed almost instantaneously.
- (C) Diffusion will take place faster if the water is colder.
- (D) Maximum color in liquid originates from the bottom of the flask.
