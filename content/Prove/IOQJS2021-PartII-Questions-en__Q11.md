# IOQJS 2021 (Part II) — Problema 11

Ramen collected rain water and measured its electrical conductivity. He boiled the water for a few minutes. Then he covered the container and allowed the water to cool to room temperature. Electrical conductivity of water now measured was lower than that measured before boiling. The reason for this most likely is:

- (A) precipitation of CaCO₃ from the water during boiling.
- (B) removal of dissolved oxygen from the water.
- (C) removal of dissolved carbon dioxide from the water.
- (D) reaction of cationic species in the water with atmospheric oxygen.
