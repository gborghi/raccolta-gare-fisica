# IOQJS 2021 (Part II) — Problema 13

**Section II.** [3 marks] A student was given 2.89 g of a mixture containing anhydrous MgCl₂ and KNO₃, and had to quantify the amount of MgCl₂ in the mixture. The student uses excess AgNO₃(aq) to precipitate the chloride ion as AgCl(s), and finds the mass of the AgCl precipitate to be 5.32 g. Calculate the mass percentage of MgCl₂ in the original mixture. (Atomic masses should be taken as per the data given.)
