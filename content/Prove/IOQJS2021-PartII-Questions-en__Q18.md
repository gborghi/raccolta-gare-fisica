**18.1.** The organs most likely belong to: (choose from the options) cockroach, prawn, tadpole, and rabbit?

The Fick's law of diffusion describes how the surface area influences the rate of diffusion and is represented as:

$$Q = D\,A\,(P1 - P2)\,/\,L$$

Where: *Q* = rate at which a gas such as O‚ÇÇ diffuses between two locations
*D* = diffusion coefficient, which is characteristic of the diffusing substance (e.g., a gas), the medium and the temperature gradient
*A* = cross sectional area over which the gas is diffusing
*P1* and *P2* are the partial pressures of the gas at the two locations
*L* = path length or distance between the two locations
