# IOQJS 2022 (Part II) — Problema 1

Neil Bartlett reacted molecular oxygen ($\text{O}_2$) with $\text{PtF}_6$ to get a compound $\text{O}_2^+\,\text{PtF}_6^-$. He repeated the experiment with xenon (Xe) in place of $\text{O}_2$ to get another substance which was found to be a mixture of compounds with two of them being $\text{XeF}^+\,\text{PtF}_6^-$ and $\text{Xe}^+\,\text{PtF}_6^-$.

Based on the above information, the statement that is true is

- (a) second ionization potential of Xe is much lower than its first ionization potential.
- (b) first ionization potential of Xe is much lower than first ionization potential of $\text{O}_2$.
- (c) Xe makes ionic bond with F in one of the above compounds.
- (d) Xe acts as reducing agent in above reactions.
