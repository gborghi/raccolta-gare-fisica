# IOQJS 2022 (Part II) — Problema 4

An industrial process uses NaCl, $\text{CaCO}_3$, $\text{H}_2$, and $\text{N}_2$ as raw materials to obtain $\text{NaHCO}_3$, using the following processes involving heat and catalysts

$$3\text{H}_2\,(\text{g}) + \text{N}_2\,(\text{g}) \to 2\text{NH}_3\,(\text{g})$$
$$\text{CaCO}_3\,(\text{s}) \to \text{CaO}\,(\text{s}) + \text{CO}_2\,(\text{g})$$
$$\text{NaCl}\,(\text{aq}) + \text{H}_2\text{O}\,(\text{l}) + \text{CO}_2\,(\text{g}) + \text{NH}_3\,(\text{g}) \to \text{NaHCO}_3\,(\text{s}) + \text{NH}_4\text{Cl}\,(\text{aq})$$

Of the following substances present in this system, those which can be heated together in another separate chamber to reduce consumption of primary raw material in this process are

- (a) $\text{H}_2$, $\text{CaCO}_3$
- (b) $\text{NH}_4\text{Cl}$, $\text{H}_2$
- (c) $\text{CaO}$, $\text{NH}_4\text{Cl}$, $\text{H}_2\text{O}$
- (d) $\text{CaCO}_3$, $\text{NH}_4\text{Cl}$
