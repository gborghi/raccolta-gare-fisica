# NSEA 2023 — Problema 12

A uniform magnetic field of strength $B = 0.1\,\text{T}$ is set up across the plane of the coil in the north-south direction. The coil is rotating about a vertical axis ($\hat{k}$) through its center at the rate of one rotation per 4 second.
