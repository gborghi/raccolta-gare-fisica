# NSEA 2023 — Problema 14

A Red Giant star of mass $M_1$ and a White Dwarf star of mass $M_2$ form a binary system and are rotating in circular orbits around their common centre of mass with a period of one year. The White Dwarf is orbiting just within the photosphere of the Red Giant star. The Red Giant has a radius of 3.6 AU. An estimate for the mass of the Red Giant star in solar mass units is (note that $M_1 \gg M_2$)

- (a) 188
- (b) 94
- (c) 47
- (d) 13
