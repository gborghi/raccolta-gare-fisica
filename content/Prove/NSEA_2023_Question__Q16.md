# NSEA 2023 — Problema 16

The rotation curve of a spiral galaxy gives the 'instantaneous' local mean tangential velocity in the plane of the galaxy of stars/gas clouds in the galaxy lying along a line through the centre of the galaxy, with respect to the distant quasars as observed from the centre of the galaxy. The following is a schematic diagram of the rotation curve of a particular spiral galaxy (flat curve beyond 6 kpc).
