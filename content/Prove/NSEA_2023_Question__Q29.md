# NSEA 2023 — Problema 29

The logistic map iteratively maps $x_n$ to $x_{n+1}$ through the equation $x_{n+1} = \lambda x_n(1 - x_n)$. Consider the two different initial values: $x_0 = 0.4$ and $x_0' = 0.4 + 0.01$. For $\lambda = 3.25$, the difference between the second iterates $(x_2 - x_2')$ is approximately

- (a) 0.01
- (b) $-0.01$
- (c) 0.02
- (d) 0.001
