# NSEA 2023 — Problema 30

In a class of 40 students 25 have opted Hindi as second language (event $A$). 35 students got first class (event $B$) in the examinations of which 20 had taken Hindi as second language. It is found that a student picked at random has secured first class. The probability $P(A/B)$ that this student has taken Hindi as second language is

- (a) $\dfrac{4}{7}$
- (b) $\dfrac{5}{8}$
- (c) $\dfrac{7}{8}$
- (d) $\dfrac{1}{2}$
