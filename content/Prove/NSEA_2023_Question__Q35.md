# NSEA 2023 — Problema 35

Five positive charges, each of magnitude $q$, are arranged symmetrically on the circumference of a circle of radius $r$. The magnitude of the electric field $E$ at the center of the circle is

- (a) Zero
- (b) $\dfrac{q}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r^2}$
- (c) $\dfrac{5q}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r^2}$
- (d) $\dfrac{\sqrt{5}\,q}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r^2}$
