# NSEA 2023 — Problema 36

A particle is constrained to move along a circle of radius $R = 1.0$ m. At a certain instant of time, the speed of the particle is $1.0$ m/s and the speed is increasing at the rate of $1.0$ m/s$^2$. The angle (in radian) between the velocity and the acceleration vectors of the particle is

- (a) Zero
- (b) $\dfrac{\pi}{6}$
- (c) $\dfrac{\pi}{4}$
- (d) $\dfrac{\pi}{2}$
