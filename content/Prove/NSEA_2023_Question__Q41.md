# NSEA 2023 — Problema 41

Two stones of equal mass are thrown vertically up simultaneously from the edge of a cliff 400 m high with initial speeds of $v_1 = 10$ m/s and $v_2 = 50$ m/s respectively. Which of the following graphs best represents the time variation of relative position $[h_2 - h_1]$ of the second stone with respect to the first? (Assume that the stones are not obstructed at the cliff while going down and do not rebound after hitting the ground. Also ignore the small horizontal separation between the stones as well as air resistance, take $g = 10$ m/s$^2$)
