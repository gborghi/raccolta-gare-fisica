# NSEA 2023 — Problema 45

A gas in equilibrium attains a state of equipartition, wherein every active degree of freedom of every particle has on the average the same energy. In particular, the average translational kinetic energy will be the same for every particle; it will be equal to $\dfrac{3}{2}kT$ where $k$ is the Boltzmann constant and $T$ is the temperature of the gas in kelvin. Consider air at 300 kelvin. If the root mean square velocity of nitrogen molecules is $v$ m/s, the magnitude of the mean velocity and the root mean square velocity of oxygen molecules are respectively

- (a) $\left(0,\, v\right)$
- (b) $\left(0,\, 0.94\,v\right)$
- (c) $\left(\sqrt{\dfrac{8kT}{\pi m_N}},\, 0.875\,v\right)$
- (d) $\left(0,\, 0.875\,v\right)$
