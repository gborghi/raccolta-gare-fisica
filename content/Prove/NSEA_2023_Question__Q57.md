# NSEA 2023 — Problema 57

An artificial satellite is moving in an orbit around the Earth with orbital period of 8 hours, from west to east, with its orbit making an angle of $30°$ with equator. An observer on the equator will see it

- (a) again after 6 hour, moving from east to west
- (b) again after about 11 hours, moving from west to east
- (c) going up to about $46.8°$ from the equatorial plane
- (d) going up to about $43.2°$ from the equatorial plane
