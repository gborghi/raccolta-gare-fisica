# NSEA 2023 — Problema 60

Two positively charged conducting spheres $A$ and $B$ with $+Q$ charge on each, are placed on the $x$-axis at $(a, 0)$ and $(-a, 0)$. A very small metallic ball with charge $+q$ is gently placed at the origin (midway between the two spheres). The small ball with charge $+q$, in the middle, is

- (a) in stable equilibrium if constrained to move only along $x$-axis
- (b) in unstable equilibrium if constrained to move only perpendicular to the $x$-axis
- (c) always in unstable equilibrium
- (d) always in neutral equilibrium
