# NSEA 2024 — Problema 29

Consider the function

$$f(x) = \begin{cases} 1 + 2x - 3x^2, & x \leq 2024 \\ 3 + 2x - x^2, & x > 2024 \end{cases}$$

Which of the following is not correct?

- (a) $f$ is discontinuous at $x = 2024$
- (b) $f$ has removable discontinuity at $x = 2024$
- (c) $f$ is continuous at $c$ where $c \neq 2024$
- (d) $f$ is differentiable at $c$ where $c \neq 2024$
