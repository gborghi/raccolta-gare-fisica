# NSEA 2024 — Problema 42

A proton moving with speed of 20 km/s enters a 200 cm long and 2.0 cm radius solenoid close to its axis. Solenoid has magnetic field of 200 gauss. If it leaves from the other end of the solenoid in 200 μs, how many revolutions can it complete (approximately) while inside the solenoid (neglect end effects)?

- (a) 20
- (b) 61
- (c) 102
- (d) 122
