# NSEA 2024 — Problema 55

Consider the tangents to the curve $y = (x-1)(x-2)(x-3)$ at the points where it meets the X-axis. Which of the following is/are true?

- (a) Two of the tangents are parallel to each other
- (b) Two of the tangents are perpendicular to each other
- (c) One of the tangents makes angle $-45°$ with the positive X-axis
- (d) Sum of the Y-intercepts made by the three tangents is $-6$
