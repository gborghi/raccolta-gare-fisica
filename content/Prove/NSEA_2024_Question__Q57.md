# NSEA 2024 — Problema 57

There are two whales of exactly the same shape and size. One can just float in a sea somewhat below the arctic circle (say arctic whale) and the other in a tropical sea near the equator (say equatorial whale). Salinity of sea water first increases in going from the equator towards the poles and then decreases in going close to the poles. Which of the following statement(s) is/are true?

- (a) The two whales are of the same mass
- (b) Equatorial whale has smaller mass
- (c) Equatorial whale has larger mass
- (d) Arctic whale has more weight
