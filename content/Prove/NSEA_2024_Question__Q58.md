# NSEA 2024 — Problema 58

A point charge $q = 1.00$ μC and mass $m = 1.00$ μg moves in a potential $V(x) = \dfrac{a}{x^2} + \dfrac{b}{x^3}$ ($x > 0$), where $a = 1000$ V·μm² and $b = 100$ V·μm³. The correct statement(s) is/are

- (a) There is an equilibrium at $x = 0.15$ μm
- (b) Around $x = 0.15$ μm, charge can make small oscillations
- (c) The period of small oscillations of charge around $x = 0.15$ is 0.10 ms
- (d) A negative charge will not experience zero force at $x = 0.15$ μm
