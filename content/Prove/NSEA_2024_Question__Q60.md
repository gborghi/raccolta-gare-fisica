# NSEA 2024 — Problema 60

Assume that the Moon's orbit around the Earth is an almost circular ellipse and that neither its orbit nor its rotation axis precesses. From the Earth we can see more than 50% of the surface of the Moon because

- (a) rotation period and revolution period of the Moon are not equal
- (b) the Moon's speed of revolution increases and decreases along the elliptical orbit
- (c) inclination of the Moon's orbit with the ecliptic plane makes the Moon alternately show more northern or more southern part
- (d) other side of the Moon is also visible sometimes
