# NSEA 2025 — Problema 1

In a hypothetical scenario the force experienced by a particle is given by $F = A\, e^{\frac{px}{t^2}} + B V_t^2$, where $V_t = \sqrt{\dfrac{mg}{\rho S \eta}}$ is the expression for terminal velocity. Where $m$ is the mass, $g$ is the acceleration due to gravity, $\rho$ is the density of the medium, $S$ is the surface area of the particle, and $\eta$ is the coefficient of viscosity of the medium. The possible units of $A$, $B$ and $p$ are

- (a) A: newton, B: newton kg m$^{-1}$, p: m s$^2$
- (b) A: no units, B: newton kg m$^{-1}$, p: m$^{-1}$ s$^2$
- (c) A: newton, B: kg m$^{-1}$, p: no units
- (d) A: newton, B: newton s$^2$ m$^{-2}$, p: m$^{-1}$ s$^2$
