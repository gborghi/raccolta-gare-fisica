# NSEA 2025 — Problema 3

Consider two telescopes having equal apertures of 400 mm. One has a focal ratio of $f/5$, the other has the focal ratio of $f/10$. What is the relation between their focal lengths?

- (a) Focal length of the telescope with $f/5$ ratio is twice as compared to the focal length of telescope with $f/10$ ratio.
- (b) Focal length of the telescope with $f/10$ ratio is twice as compared to the focal length of telescope with $f/5$ ratio.
- (c) Focal length of the telescope with $f/10$ ratio is four times smaller as compared to the focal length of telescope with $f/5$ ratio.
- (d) Focal lengths of both telescopes are the same.
