# NSEA 2025 — Problema 4

Statement I: The radius vector of a planet sweeps equal area in equal time while revolving around the Sun.

Statement II: Gravitational force between the Sun and the planet is along the line joining the two.

- (a) Both the Statements are true, and Statement II is correct reason of Statement I.
- (b) Both the Statements are true, but Statement II is not the correct reason of Statement I.
- (c) The Statement I is true and the Statement II is false.
- (d) The Statement I is false and the Statement II is true.
