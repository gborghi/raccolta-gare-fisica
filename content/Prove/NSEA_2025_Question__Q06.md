# NSEA 2025 — Problema 6

Three identical bulbs, B, C and D are connected in a circuit as shown below. By connecting one more identical bulb

- (a) in series with bulb B, the intensity of bulb B will decrease but that of bulb C and D will increase.
- (b) in parallel with bulb B, the intensities of all the three bulbs B, C and D will decrease.
- (c) in parallel with bulbs C and D, the intensities of all the three bulbs B, C and D will increase.
- (d) in series with bulb C, the intensity of bulb B will decrease and that of bulb D will increase.
