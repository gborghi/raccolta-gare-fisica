# NSEA 2025 — Problema 11

A mechanical spring deviates from Hooke's law as $F = k(e^x - 1)$, where $x$ is the strain. At what value of the strain $x$, the force deviates from the one obeying Hooke's law with same $k$ and unit length, just by 1%?

- (a) $x \approx 1\%$
- (b) $x \approx 2\%$
- (c) $x \approx 5\%$
- (d) $x \approx 20\%$
