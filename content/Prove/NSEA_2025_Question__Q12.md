# NSEA 2025 — Problema 12

Three lenses with focal lengths $f$, $f'$ and $f$ respectively are kept with common principal axis in that order with successive separation of $\dfrac{f}{2}$ between each pair. If the point object is placed on the common principal axis to the left of 1st lens at $2f$ distance from it. What should be the value of focal length $f'$ so as to form the final image at distance of $2f$ from 3rd lens on the right of it?

- (a) $-\dfrac{4}{3}f$
- (b) $\dfrac{4}{3}f$
- (c) $-\dfrac{3}{4}f$
- (d) $-\dfrac{3}{5}f$
