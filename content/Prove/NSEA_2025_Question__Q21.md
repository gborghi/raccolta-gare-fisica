# NSEA 2025 — Problema 21

- (a) $f$ is differentiable everywhere
- (b) $f$ is continuous everywhere but not differentiable at $x = \pm 1$
- (c) $f$ is not continuous at $x = \pm 1$
- (d) $f$ is neither continuous nor differentiable
