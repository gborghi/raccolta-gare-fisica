# NSEA 2025 — Problema 28

A water tank has the shape of an inverted right circular cone, whose semi-vertical angle is $\tan^{-1}\!\left(\dfrac{3}{4}\right)$. Water is poured into it at a constant rate of 5 m$^3$/min. Then, the rate (in m/min) at which the water surface moves along the slant surface at the instant when the depth of water in the tank is 10 m is

- (a) $\dfrac{1}{9\pi}$
- (b) $\dfrac{9}{192\pi}$
- (c) $\dfrac{1875}{4\pi}$
- (d) $\dfrac{4}{27\pi}$
