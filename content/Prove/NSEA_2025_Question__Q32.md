# NSEA 2025 — Problema 32

Let $A = \begin{pmatrix} a & b & c \\ b & c & a \\ c & a & b \end{pmatrix}$. Which of the following statements is not true?

- (a) $A$ is symmetric
- (b) If $a + b + c = 0$ then $\det A = 0$
- (c) $\det A = a^3 + b^3 + c^3 - 3abc$
- (d) $\det A = \det A^T$
