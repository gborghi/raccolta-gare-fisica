# NSEA 2025 — Problema 52

Two black bodies A and B are emitting in the approximate ratio 2:5. Which of the following statements may be correct from the given information?

- (a) Body B is 20% hotter and 10% larger in diameter than body A.
- (b) Body B is 50% hotter but half in diameter than body A.
- (c) Body B is 10% hotter and 30% larger in diameter than body A.
- (d) Body B has double the temperature of A and only 40% diameter of body A.
