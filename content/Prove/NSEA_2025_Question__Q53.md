# NSEA 2025 — Problema 53

Let $\delta = \begin{vmatrix} x & x^2 & 1 \\ 1 & x & x^2 \\ x^2 & 1 & x \end{vmatrix}$. Which of the following statements is not true?

- (a) $\delta$ is an even function
- (b) $\delta = 0$ for all real $x$
- (c) $\delta$ is a polynomial of degree 6
- (d) If $x = \sqrt{\dfrac{1}{3}}$, $\delta = 0$ for all roots
