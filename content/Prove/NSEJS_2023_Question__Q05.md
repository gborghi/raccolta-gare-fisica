# NSEJS 2023 — Problema 5

Phenylthiocarbamide (PTC) has a bitter taste. Non-tasting ability is reported to be due to recessive allele of the taste gene. In random populations about 30% people lack the ability to taste PTC. A non-taster woman is married to a PTC taster man and has three children. The first two children are born as non-tasters. What is the probability that their third child will be born a non-taster?

- (a) 0.25
- (b) 0.50
- (c) 0.15
- (d) 0.75
