# NSEJS 2023 — Problema 58

Two tungsten filament bulbs with rating 100 W, 200 V and 60 W, 200 V are connected in series with a variable supply of 0–400 V range, as shown. The supply voltage is gradually increased from 0 to 400 V. Choose the correct statement(s).

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 58**
![[NSEJS_2023_Question_p10_f12.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) When supply voltage is 200 V, 60 W bulb glows brighter
- (b) When supply voltage is 200 V, total power dissipated in both the bulbs is greater than 37.5 W
- (c) When the supply voltage is 400 V, the 100 W bulb gets fused
- (d) When supply voltage becomes 400 V, none of the bulbs glow
