# NSEJS 2023 — Problema 59

A solid sphere of radius $R = 10\,\text{cm}$ floats in water with 60% of its volume submerged. In oil, this sphere floats with 80% of its volume submerged. If the density of water is $1000\,\text{kg/m}^3$. The correct statement(s) is/are that

- (a) the density of the material of sphere is 600 kg/m³
- (b) the density of the oil is 750 kg/m³
- (c) the weight of the sphere in air is close to 24.64 N
- (d) the loss in weight of the sphere when floating in oil is close to 30.82 N
