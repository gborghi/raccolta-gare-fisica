# NSEJS 2024 — Problema 19

One structural isomer of $\text{C}_2\text{H}_4\text{I}_2$ contains both the Iodine (I) atoms attached to same carbon atom. The bond length of C–I bond is 2.10 Å. Assuming tetrahedral angles and additivity of covalent bond radii, the distance between two Iodine (I) atoms is (given that $\sin 54.75° = 0.815$ and $\sin 109.5° = 0.94$)

- (a) 2.10 Å
- (b) 2.47 Å
- (c) 4.20 Å
- (d) 3.42 Å
