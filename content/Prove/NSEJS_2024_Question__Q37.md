# NSEJS 2024 — Problema 37

A particle is moving along circular path of diameter $D = 14$ cm with constant speed $v$. It takes 0.02 second to complete an arc which subtends an angle of 45° at the center. If $f$ is its frequency of revolution, then the correct option is

- (a) $v = 5.5\,\text{m/s}$, $f = 6.25\,\text{Hz}$
- (b) $v = 5.5\,\text{m/s}$, $f = 12.5\,\text{Hz}$
- (c) $v = 2.75\,\text{m/s}$, $f = 6.25\,\text{Hz}$
- (d) $v = 2.75\,\text{m/s}$, $f = 12.5\,\text{Hz}$
