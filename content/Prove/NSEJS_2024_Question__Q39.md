# NSEJS 2024 — Problema 39

An ant moves at constant speed on the principal axis of concave mirror of focal length $f$, from a point at distance $5f$ from the pole of the mirror to the focus $F$ of the mirror. During the motion of the ant (consider the ant as a point), its image formed by the mirror:

$S_1$: moves with constant speed
$S_2$: has same velocity as that of the ant when the ant is at center of curvature $C$ of the mirror
$S_3$: moves slower in the beginning and faster towards the end
$S_4$: moves faster in the beginning and slower towards the end

Now, choose the correct option:

- (a) Only the statement $S_1$ is correct
- (b) Statements $S_2$ and $S_3$ are correct
- (c) Only the statement $S_3$ is correct
- (d) The statements $S_2$ and $S_4$ are correct
