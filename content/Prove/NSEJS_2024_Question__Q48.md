# NSEJS 2024 — Problema 48

The mass and radius of planet-one are $M$ and $R$ respectively. The mass and radius of planet-two are $2M$ and $2R$ respectively. Assume that both the planets have spherical shape. Acceleration due to gravity on the surface of planet-one and planet-two are $g_1$ and $g_2$ respectively. $\dfrac{g_1}{g_2}$ must be

- (a) 1 : 2
- (b) 2 : 1
- (c) 1 : 4
- (d) 4 : 1
