# NSEJS 2025 — Problema 33

A student, intending to catch a bus on the bus stand, finds that the bus starts and accelerates away from him/her with uniform acceleration $a = 2\,\text{ms}^{-2}$ when he is 16 m far from the bus. He runs fast enough with a uniform speed so as just to catch the bus. The minimum speed with which the student must run, is

- (a) $2.0\,\text{ms}^{-1}$
- (b) $4.0\,\text{ms}^{-1}$
- (c) $8.0\,\text{ms}^{-1}$
- (d) $16.0\,\text{ms}^{-1}$
