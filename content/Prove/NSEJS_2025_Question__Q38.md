# NSEJS 2025 — Problema 38

An electric kettle consists of two filaments $F_1$ and $F_2$ of different resistances $R_1$ and $R_2$ respectively, connected in parallel with separate switches. With a certain fixed amount of water in the kettle pot to prepare the tea, when only $F_1$ is switched on, it takes 4 minutes to boil the water. When $F_1$ and $F_2$ both are switched on simultaneously, it takes 3 minutes to boil the same amount of water up to the same temperature. The time taken to boil the same amount of water when only $F_2$ is switched on (assume that entire heat radiated by filaments is used in boiling the water starting from the same room temperature in each case) is

- (a) 7.0 min
- (b) 8.4 min
- (c) 9.6 min
- (d) 12.0 min
