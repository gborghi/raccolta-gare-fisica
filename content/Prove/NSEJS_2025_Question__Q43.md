# NSEJS 2025 — Problema 43

A stone is thrown vertically down into a well, the splash is heard after a time $t_1$. If the stone is thrown vertically up with the same speed from the same point, the splash is heard after a time $t_2$. The common initial speed $u$ of the stone in both the cases is given by (the time taken by the splash sound to reach the observer is ignored):

- (a) $u = g(t_2 - t_1)$
- (b) $u = g(t_1 - t_2)$
- (c) $u = \dfrac{1}{2}g(t_2 - t_1)$
- (d) $u = \dfrac{1}{2}g(t_2 + t_1)$
