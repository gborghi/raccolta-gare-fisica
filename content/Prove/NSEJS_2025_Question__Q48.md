# NSEJS 2025 — Problema 48

A steam boat goes across a lake up to a distance of 1200 m and comes back. There is a uniform wind blowing with velocity $v$ so as to help the onward journey and to impede the journey back. If the uniform speed of the boat is $5\,\text{ms}^{-1}$ and total time taken by the boat in going and coming back is 500 s, the velocity $v$ of the wind blow is

- (a) $0.6\,\text{ms}^{-1}$
- (b) $0.8\,\text{ms}^{-1}$
- (c) $1.0\,\text{ms}^{-1}$
- (d) $1.2\,\text{ms}^{-1}$
