# NSEP 2023 — Problema 1

**Part A-1 (only one out of four options is correct).**

Two balls are projected from the top of a cliff with equal initial speed $u$. One starts at angle $\theta$ above the horizontal while the other starts at angle $\theta$ below. Difference in their ranges on ground is

- (a) $\dfrac{u^2 \tan\theta}{g}$
- (b) $\dfrac{u^2 \sin 2\theta}{2g}$
- (c) $\dfrac{u^2 \sin 2\theta}{g}$
- (d) $\dfrac{u^2 \cos 2\theta}{g}$
