# NSEP 2023 — Problema 9

The density of a liquid is $\rho$ at the surface. The bulk modulus of the liquid is B. The increase $\Delta\rho$ in the density of the liquid at a depth $h$ from the surface is (with $\Delta\rho \ll \rho$)

- (a) $\Delta\rho = \dfrac{\rho^2 g h}{B}$
- (b) $\Delta\rho = \dfrac{\rho g h}{B}$
- (c) $\Delta\rho = \dfrac{\rho^2 g h}{2B}$
- (d) $\Delta\rho = \dfrac{2\rho^2 g h}{B}$
