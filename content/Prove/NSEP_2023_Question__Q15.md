# NSEP 2023 — Problema 15

The pan is connected at the end of a horizontal un-stretched (relaxed) spring of force constant K = 32 Nm$^{-1}$ as shown in figure. After the block collides, the displacement $x(t)$ of the block as a function of time $t$ is given by
