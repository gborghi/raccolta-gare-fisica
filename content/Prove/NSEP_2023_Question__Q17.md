# NSEP 2023 — Problema 17

Two Carnot heat engines are connected in series such that the sink of the first engine is heat source of the second. Efficiency of the engines are $\eta_1$ and $\eta_2$ respectively. Net efficiency $\eta$ of the combination is given by

- (a) $\eta = \eta_1 + \eta_2$
- (b) $\eta = \dfrac{\eta_1 \eta_2}{\eta_1 + \eta_2}$
- (c) $\eta = \eta_1 + \eta_2(1 - \eta_1)$
- (d) $\eta = \eta_1 - \eta_2(1 - \eta_1)$
