# NSEP 2023 — Problema 19

Two soap bubbles of radii $a$ and $b$ coalesce to form a single bubble of radius $c$ under isothermal conditions. If the external pressure is $P_A$, then the Surface Tension (T) of the soap solution is

- (a) $\dfrac{P_A}{4}\dfrac{(c^3 - a^3 - b^3)}{(a^2 + b^2 - c^2)}$
- (b) $\dfrac{P_A}{2}\dfrac{(a^3 + b^3 - c^3)}{(c^2 - a^2 - b^2)}$
- (c) $\dfrac{P_A}{2}\dfrac{(a^2 + b^2 - c^2)}{(c^3 - a^3 - b^3)}$
- (d) $\dfrac{P_A}{4}\dfrac{(c^2 - a^2 - b^2)}{(a + b - c)}$
