# NSEP 2023 — Problema 22

An ideal gas ($n$ moles) is initially at pressure P and temperature T. It is cooled isochorically to a pressure $\dfrac{P}{4}$. The gas is then expanded at a constant pressure so as to attain back its initial temperature T. Work done by the gas in the whole process is

- (a) $\dfrac{3}{4}nRT$
- (b) $\dfrac{3}{4}RT$
- (c) $\dfrac{1}{4}nRT$
- (d) Zero
