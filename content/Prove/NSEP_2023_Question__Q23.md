# NSEP 2023 — Problema 23

Assuming the Sun to be a spherical body (radius $R_S$) of surface temperature T, the total radiation power received by Earth (radius $R_E$) at a distance $r$ from Sun is

- (a) $\dfrac{\sigma\pi R_E^2 R_S^2 T^4}{r^2}$
- (b) $\dfrac{\sigma 4\pi R_S^2 T^4}{r^2}$
- (c) $\dfrac{\sigma\pi R_E^2 R_S^2 T^4}{4r^2}$
- (d) $\dfrac{\sigma R_E^2 R_S^2 T^4}{4\pi r^2}$
