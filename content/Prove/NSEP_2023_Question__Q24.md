# NSEP 2023 — Problema 24

The figure shows five point-charges on a straight line. Separation between successive charges is 10 cm. For what values of $q_1$ and $q_2$ would the net force on each of the other three charges be zero?

- (a) $q_1 = q_2 = -\dfrac{27}{80}\ \mu\text{C}$
- (b) $q_1 = q_2 = -\dfrac{27}{40}\ \mu\text{C}$
- (c) $q_1 = \dfrac{27}{80}\ \mu\text{C},\ q_2 = -\dfrac{27}{80}\ \mu\text{C}$
- (d) $q_1 = q_2 = \dfrac{27}{40}\ \mu\text{C}$
