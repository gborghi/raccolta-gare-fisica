# NSEP 2023 — Problema 26

A very small electric dipole of dipole moment $\vec{p}$ lies along the x axis $\left(\text{i.e. } \vec{p} = p\,\hat{i}\right)$ in a non-uniform electric field $\vec{E} = \dfrac{c}{x}\,\hat{i}$ (where c is a constant). The force on the dipole is

- (a) $\dfrac{c\,p}{x^2}\,\hat{i}$
- (b) $-\dfrac{c\,p}{x^2}\,\hat{i}$
- (c) $\dfrac{c\,p}{x}\,\hat{i}$
- (d) zero
