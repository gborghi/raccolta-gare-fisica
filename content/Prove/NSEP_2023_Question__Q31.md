# NSEP 2023 — Problema 31

The two ends of the wire are immersed in mercury for proper electrical contact. The wire is in a homogeneous field of magnetic induction $B = 0.1\ \text{T}$ as shown. The wire jumps up to a height $h = 3\ \text{m}$ when a charge q, in the form of a current pulse, is sent through the wire.
