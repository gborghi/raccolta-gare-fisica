# NSEP 2023 — Problema 32

A target of $^7$Li is bombarded with a proton beam of current $10^{-4}$ ampere for 1 hour to produce $^7$Be of activity $1.8 \times 10^8$ disintegrations per second. Assuming that bombarding of 1000 protons produces one $^7$Be radioactive nucleus, the half-life of $^7$Be is estimated to be approximately

- (a) 6887 hour
- (b) 4332 hour
- (c) 2407 hour
- (d) 2195 hour
