# NSEP 2023 — Problema 44

The convex surface of a concavo-convex lens of refractive index 1.5 and radii of curvature $R_1 = 20$ cm and $R_2 = 40$ cm has been silvered so as to make it reflecting. The distance of a luminous object from the reflecting system when placed in front of it on its principal axis, so that the image coincides with the object is

- (a) 40 cm
- (b) 32 cm
- (c) 16 cm
- (d) 8 cm
