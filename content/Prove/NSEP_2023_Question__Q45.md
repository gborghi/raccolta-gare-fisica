# NSEP 2023 — Problema 45

A direct vision spectroscope has been designed to obtain dispersion without deviation by arranging alternate inverted thin prisms of crown glass (refractive index $\mu_1 = \sqrt{2}$) and flint glass ($\mu_2 = \sqrt{3}$) with refracting angle $\theta_{\text{flint}} = 3^\circ$. The refracting angle $\theta_{\text{crown}}$ of the crown glass prism is

- (a) $3.0^\circ$
- (b) $4.5^\circ$
- (c) $5.3^\circ$
- (d) $6.0^\circ$
