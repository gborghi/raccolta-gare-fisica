# NSEP 2023 — Problema 46

X-rays are produced when electron beam accelerated by a high voltage V is made to hit the metallic target such as Molybdenum in a X-ray tube. $\lambda_{\min}$ is the shortest possible wavelength of continuous X-rays and $\lambda_{L\alpha}$ is the wavelength of characteristic X-rays. Then

- (a) $\lambda_{\min}$ decreases with increase in V
- (b) $\lambda_{L\alpha}$ decreases with increase in V
- (c) $\lambda_{L\alpha}$ is independent of V
- (d) $\lambda_{\min}$ decreases with increase in V
