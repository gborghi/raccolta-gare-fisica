# NSEP 2023 — Problema 54

Two equal positive charges +Q each lie on y axis at $(0, a)$ and $(0, -a)$. The electric field strength E at a point $(x, 0)$ satisfies:

- (a) $E = \dfrac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\dfrac{2Qa}{(x^2 + a^2)^{3/2}}$
- (b) for large values of $x$ ($i.e\ x \gg a$), the electric field $E \propto \dfrac{1}{x^2}$
- (c) for $x \ge 0$, E is maximum at $x = \dfrac{a}{\sqrt{2}}$
- (d) for $x \ge 0$, E is maximum at $x = 0$ and is equal to $\dfrac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\dfrac{2Q}{a^2}$
