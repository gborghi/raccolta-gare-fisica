# NSEP 2023 — Problema 55

A metal rod of mass m and length $\ell$ slides on frictionless parallel metal rails of negligible resistance. A resistance R is connected between the rails at their ends as shown in the figure. A uniform magnetic field B is directed into the plane of paper perpendicular to the plane of rails throughout the space. The rod is given an initial velocity $v_0$ (towards right). No other force acts on the rod. Then

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 55**
![[NSEP_2023_Question_p12_f1.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) $v(t) = v_0 e^{\frac{-B\ell t}{mR}}$
- (b) the rod stops after traveling a distance $x = \dfrac{m v_0 R}{B^2 \ell^2}$
- (c) the total energy dissipated in resistance is $\dfrac{1}{4}mv_0^2$ i.e. half of the initial kinetic energy
- (d) the total charge that flows in the circuit is $q = \dfrac{m v_0}{B\ell}$
