# NSEP 2023 — Problema 58

In an experiment with Lummer Gehrecke plate, the two coherent beams of light, caused by multiple reflections inside the transparent plate of refractive index $\mu = 1.54$, reach the points P and Q on the screen. The net path difference between the two beams reaching either at P or Q is $\Delta x = 5000\ nm$. Which of the wavelengths in the visible range $(\lambda = 390\ nm$ to $\lambda = 780\ nm)$ are most likely to produce a constructive interference (a maximum) at the point P as well as at Q on the screen?
