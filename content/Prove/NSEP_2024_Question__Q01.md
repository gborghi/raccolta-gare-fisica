# NSEP 2024 — Problema 1

In the Bohr model of hydrogen atom, the force between the nucleus and the electron is modified as $F = \dfrac{e^2}{4\pi\,\epsilon_0}\left(\dfrac{1}{r^2} + \dfrac{\delta}{r^3}\right)$ where $\delta$ is a small constant. Using the Bohr radius $a_0 = \dfrac{\epsilon_0\,h^2}{\pi m e^2}$, the radius of $n^{th}$ orbit is

- (a) $a_0\,n^2 - \delta$
- (b) $a_0\,n^2 + \delta$
- (c) $a_0\,(n - \delta)^2$
- (d) $a_0\,(n + \delta)^2$
