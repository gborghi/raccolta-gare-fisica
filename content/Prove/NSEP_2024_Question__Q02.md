# NSEP 2024 — Problema 2

An infinite number of conducting rings having increasing radii $r_0, r_1, r_2, r_3$ and so on, such that $r_0 = r$, $r_1 = 2r$, $r_2 = 3r$, $r_3 = 4r$ and so on ... upto $\infty$, have been placed concentrically on a plane. All the rings carry the same current $I$ but the current in consecutive rings is in opposite direction as shown. The magnetic field produced at the common center of the rings is
