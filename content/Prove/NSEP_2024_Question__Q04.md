# NSEP 2024 — Problema 4

A spring is compressed under the action of a constant force $F$. The compression of the spring is $\xi$. Now the force $F$ is reversed suddenly as well as its magnitude is doubled. The maximum extension of the spring beyond its natural length will now be (the spring obeys Hook's law).

- (a) $2\,\xi$
- (b) $3\,\xi$
- (c) $4\,\xi$
- (d) $5\,\xi$
