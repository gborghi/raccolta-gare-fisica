# NSEP 2024 — Problema 5

Two small charged balls are suspended from a common point at the ceiling by two non-conducting massless strings of equal length $\ell$. The first ball has mass $m_1$ and charge $q_1$ while the second ball has mass $m_2$ and charge $q_2$. If the two strings subtend angles $\theta_1$ and $\theta_2$ with the vertical as shown, then

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 5**
![[NSEP_2024_Question_p3_f2.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) $\dfrac{\sin\theta_1}{\sin\theta_2} = \dfrac{m_1}{m_2}$
- (b) $\dfrac{\sin\theta_1}{\sin\theta_2} = \dfrac{m_2}{m_1}$
- (c) $\dfrac{\tan\theta_1}{\tan\theta_2} = \dfrac{q_2 + m_2}{q_1 + m_1}$
- (d) $\dfrac{\sin\theta_1}{\sin\theta_2} = 1$
