# NSEP 2024 — Problema 11

A uniform beam of light of intensity $60$ mW/m$^2$ is incident on a totally absorbing sphere of radius $2.0\ \mu$m. The density of the material of the sphere is $\rho = 5.0 \times 10^3$ kg/m$^3$. The sphere is placed in a region free of gravity. The acceleration of the sphere is

- (a) $1.0 \times 10^{-3}$ m s$^{-2}$
- (b) $1.5 \times 10^{-3}$ m s$^{-2}$
- (c) $0.75 \times 10^{-3}$ m s$^{-2}$
- (d) Zero
