# NSEP 2024 — Problema 14

A charged spherical capacitor consists of two concentric spherical shells of radii $a$ and $b$ ($b > a$). Half of the stored electrical energy of this system lies within a spherical region of radius $r$ if

- (a) $r = \sqrt{ab}$
- (b) $r = \dfrac{a + b}{2}$
- (c) $r = \dfrac{2ab}{a + b}$
- (d) $r = \dfrac{a^2 + b^2}{a + b}$
