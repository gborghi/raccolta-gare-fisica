# NSEP 2024 — Problema 15

Two stars of masses $M_1 = M_S$ and $M_2 = 8 M_S$ (where $M_S$ = mass of the Sun) form a binary system. The two stars being at a separation of $d = 4$ AU between them (1 AU $= 1.5 \times 10^{11}$ m). They revolve in circular orbits about their center of mass. The period of revolution is

- (a) $1$ year
- (b) $2$ years
- (c) $4$ years
- (d) $8$ years
