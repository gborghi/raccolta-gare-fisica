# NSEP 2024 — Problema 17

A listener at rest (with respect to the air and the ground) hears a sound of frequency $f_1$ from a source moving towards him with a velocity of $15$ m s$^{-1}$, towards East. If the listener now moves towards the approaching source with a velocity of $25$ m s$^{-1}$, towards West, he hears a frequency $f_2$ that differs from $f_1$ by 40 Hz. The frequency $f$ of the sound produced by the source is (speed of sound in the air is $340$ m s$^{-1}$)

- (a) 520 Hz
- (b) 450 Hz
- (c) 480 Hz
- (d) 550 Hz
