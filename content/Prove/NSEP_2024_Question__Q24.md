# NSEP 2024 — Problema 24

A cylindrical tank with base area $A = 0.05$ m$^2$ is filled with water up to a height $H = 50$ cm. There is a small hole of area $a = 0.001$ m$^2$ ($a \ll A$) in the bottom of the tank. It takes time $t$ to empty the tank up to a height $\dfrac{H}{2}$ (i.e. to empty half of the water volume). The additional time required to empty the tank completely is

- (a) $t$
- (b) $t\sqrt{2}$
- (c) $t\,(\sqrt{2} - 1)$
- (d) $t\,(\sqrt{2} + 1)$
