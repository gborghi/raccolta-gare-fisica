# NSEP 2024 — Problema 33

A Keplerian telescope is adjusted in its normal setting for parallel rays. Mounting of the objective has diameter $D$ and the diameter of the image of that mounting formed by the eye piece of the telescope is $d$. Magnifying power of the telescope is

- (a) $\dfrac{D}{d} - 1$
- (b) $\dfrac{D}{d} + 1$
- (c) $\dfrac{D + d}{D - d}$
- (d) $\dfrac{D}{d}$
