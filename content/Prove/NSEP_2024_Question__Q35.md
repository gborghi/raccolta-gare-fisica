# NSEP 2024 — Problema 35

There are two given physical quantities $A = \dfrac{F}{\rho v^2 D}$ and $B = \dfrac{\rho v D}{\eta}$ where $F$ is force, $\rho$ is density, $D$ is diameter, $v$ is velocity and $\eta$ is the Coefficient of viscosity. Which of the two A and B is/are dimensionless?

- (a) A
- (b) B
- (c) neither A nor B
- (d) both A and B
