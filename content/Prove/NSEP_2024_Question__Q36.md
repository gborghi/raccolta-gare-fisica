# NSEP 2024 — Problema 36

A uniform inextensible string of length $L$ and mass $M$ is suspended vertically from a rigid support. A transverse pulse is allowed to propagate down through the string from the support. At the same time, a ball of mass $m$ is dropped from the rigid support. The ball will pass the pulse at a distance of (from the top of the string i.e. from the support)

- (a) $\dfrac{15L}{16}$
- (b) $\dfrac{8L}{9}$
- (c) $\dfrac{L}{2}$
- (d) $\dfrac{3L}{4}$
