# NSEP 2024 — Problema 37

The maximum kinetic energy of the emitted positron (i.e. the $Q$ value) is approximately

- (a) 1.45 MeV
- (b) 0.96 MeV
- (c) 0.43 MeV
- (d) $-1.45$ MeV
