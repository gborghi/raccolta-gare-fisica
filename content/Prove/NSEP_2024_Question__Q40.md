# NSEP 2024 — Problema 40

The electrostatic potential energy $U$ is stored within the volume of a non-conducting sphere of radius $R$ carrying total charge $Q$ uniformly distributed over its volume. The total electrostatic energy contained within the sphere is $U = \alpha \times \dfrac{Q^2}{4\pi\epsilon_0 R}$. The value of $\alpha$ is

- (a) Zero
- (b) $\dfrac{3}{5}$
- (c) $\dfrac{1}{2}$
- (d) $\dfrac{1}{10}$
