# NSEP 2024 — Problema 41

A long straight vertical wire of circular cross section of radius $R$ contains $n$ conduction electrons per unit volume. A current I flows upward in the wire. The expression for magnetic force on an electron at the surface of the wire is [Assume all the conduction electrons are moving with drift velocity]

- (a) $\dfrac{\mu_0}{4\pi^2}\dfrac{I^2}{n R^3}$ outward
- (b) $\dfrac{\mu_0}{2\pi}\dfrac{I^2}{n R^3}$ inward
- (c) $\dfrac{\mu_0}{2\pi^2}\dfrac{I^2}{n R^3}$ inward
- (d) $\dfrac{\mu_0}{2\pi^2}\dfrac{I^2}{n R^3}$ outward
