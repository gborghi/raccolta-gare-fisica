# NSEP 2024 — Problema 42

In Young's double slit experiment, a bright fringe is observed at $y = 1.5$ cm from the center of the fringe pattern when monochromatic light of wavelength 612 nm is used. The screen is at 1.4 m from the plane of the two slits, whose separation is 0.4 mm. The number of dark fringes between the center and the said bright fringe at $y = 1.5$ cm is

- (a) 13
- (b) 8
- (c) 7
- (d) 6
