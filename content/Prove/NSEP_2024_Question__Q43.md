# NSEP 2024 — Problema 43

An illuminated point object is placed in front of an equi-convex lens of refractive index $\mu = 1.5$ and focal length $f = 40$ cm, at a distance of $u = 1.20$ m in front of the lens on its principal axis. Water ($\mu = 4/3$) fills the space behind the lens up to a distance of 40 cm from the lens. The final image is formed on the principal axis beyond the lens at a distance $v$ from the lens. The value of $v$ and the nature of image are

- (a) 60 cm, virtual
- (b) 80 cm, virtual
- (c) 110 cm, real
- (d) 130 cm, real
