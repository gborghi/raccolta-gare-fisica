# NSEP 2024 — Problema 52

A series LCR circuit fed with AC has resonant angular frequency of $\omega = 2.0 \times 10^4$ rad/s. When the same circuit is driven at an angular frequency of $2.5 \times 10^4$ rad/s, it has an impedance of $1.0$ k$\Omega$ and phase constant of $\phi = 45°$. The values of L, C and R for this circuit may be

- (a) $R = 707\ \Omega$
- (b) $L = 78.6$ mH
- (c) $C = 31.8$ nf
- (d) $R = 506\ \Omega$
