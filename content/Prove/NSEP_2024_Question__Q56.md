# NSEP 2024 — Problema 56

A solid ball of mass $M$ and radius $R$ (see figure) is lying on a horizontal table. The ball experiences a short horizontal impulse which imparts a momentum $p$ to the ball. The height of point of impact above the center line is $h = \alpha R$ ($0 \le \alpha \le 1$). Choose the correct option/option(s).

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 56**
![[NSEP_2024_Question_p12_f2.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) Translational energy of the ball is $\dfrac{p^2}{2M}$
- (b) Energy of pure rotational motion of the ball is $\dfrac{5}{4}\dfrac{p^2\alpha^2}{M}$
- (c) For $\alpha = \dfrac{2}{5}$, the ball rolls without sliding
- (d) For $\alpha = 0$, pure rotational motion will be observed
