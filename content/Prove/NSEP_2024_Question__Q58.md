# NSEP 2024 — Problema 58

A body of mass $m = 0.25$ kg is moving along $x$ axis under the action of a conservative force. Its potential energy as a function of position $x$ is given by $U(x) = -\dfrac{100x}{x^2 + 4}$ J ($x$ in m). Then

- (a) force $F(x)$ acting on the body at $x = 0$ is 25 N
- (b) there is stable equilibrium at $x = 2$ m
- (c) there is unstable equilibrium at $x = -2$ m
- (d) the body executes (small) oscillations with angular frequency $\omega = 0.5$ rad s$^{-1}$
