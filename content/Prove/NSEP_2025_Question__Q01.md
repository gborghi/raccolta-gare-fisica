# NSEP 2025 — Problema 1

A point mass $m$ moves in a straight line under a retardation $kv^2$ [where $k$ is a positive constant and $v$ is the instantaneous velocity]. The initial velocity of the point mass is $u$. The displacement of the point mass at time $t$ is

- (a) $\dfrac{1}{k}\ln(1+kut)$
- (b) $\dfrac{1}{k}\ln(kut)$
- (c) $k\ln(kut)$
- (d) $-\dfrac{1}{k}\ln(1+kut)$
