# NSEP 2025 — Problema 3

A thin uniform circular ring of mass $m$ is rolling without slipping down an inclined plane of inclination $30°$ with the horizontal. The coefficient of friction between the ring and the surface is $\mu$. The correct statement is

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 3**
![[NSEP_2025_Question_p3_f2.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) linear acceleration of the center of the ring along the plane is $a = \dfrac{g}{2}$
- (b) force of friction between the ring and the inclined plane is $F_\text{friction} = \dfrac{mg}{4}$
- (c) the ring keeps rolling for all values of the coefficient of friction $\mu \geq \dfrac{1}{4}$
- (d) linear acceleration of the center of the ring along the plane is $a = \dfrac{g}{3}$
