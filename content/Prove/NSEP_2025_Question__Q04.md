# NSEP 2025 — Problema 4

A bullet of mass $m$ can penetrate a target (a heavy block of mass $M$) up to a distance $S$, when the target $M$ is held stationary by a stopper P (shown in figure). Up to what distance $S'$ the bullet will penetrate if the block of mass $M$ is free to move (i.e. when the stopper P is removed) on the frictionless surface T?

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 4**
![[NSEP_2025_Question_p3_f3.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) $S' = S$
- (b) $S' = \dfrac{m}{M}S$
- (c) $S' = \dfrac{m}{m+M}S$
- (d) $S' = \dfrac{M}{M+m}S$
