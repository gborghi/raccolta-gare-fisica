# NSEP 2025 — Problema 5

$${}^{25}_{13}\text{Al} \to {}^{25}_{12}\text{Mg} + e^+ + \nu$$
in MeV is

- (a) 4.278
- (b) 3.767
- (c) 3.256
- (d) 931.478
