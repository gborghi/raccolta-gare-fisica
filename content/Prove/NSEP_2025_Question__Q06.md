# NSEP 2025 — Problema 6

A block of mass $m$, lying on a rough horizontal plane, is acted upon by a horizontal force $P$ and simultaneously by another force $Q$ acting at an angle $\theta$ from the vertical as shown. The block will remain in equilibrium if the coefficient of friction between the block and the surface $S$ is

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 6**
![[NSEP_2025_Question_p4_f4.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) at least $\dfrac{P + Q\sin\theta}{mg + Q\cos\theta}$
- (b) at least $\dfrac{P + Q\cos\theta}{mg + Q\sin\theta}$
- (c) equal to $\dfrac{P + Q\sin\theta}{mg - Q\cos\theta}$
- (d) equal to $\dfrac{P + Q\cos\theta}{mg - Q\sin\theta}$
