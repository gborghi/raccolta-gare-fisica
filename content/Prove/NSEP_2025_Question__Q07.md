# NSEP 2025 — Problema 7

Knowing that the acceleration due to gravity on the Earth surface is $g$ and the radius of the Earth is $R$, a small body of mass $m$ falls on the Earth from a height $h = \dfrac{R}{5}$ above the Earth's surface. During the freefall, the potential energy of the falling body decreases by

- (a) $mgh$
- (b) $\dfrac{4}{5}mgh$
- (c) $\dfrac{5}{6}mgh$
- (d) $\dfrac{6}{7}mgh$
