# NSEP 2025 — Problema 8

At some instant, a motor car is moving on a circular path of radius 600 m, with a speed $u = 30\ \text{ms}^{-1}$. If its speed is increased at a rate of $2\ \text{ms}^{-2}$, the magnitude of the acceleration of the car at that instant is

- (a) $2.0\ \text{ms}^{-2}$
- (b) $2.5\ \text{ms}^{-2}$
- (c) $3.5\ \text{ms}^{-2}$
- (d) $1.5\ \text{ms}^{-2}$
