# NSEP 2025 — Problema 11

A simple pendulum, with a bob of mass $m$, oscillates in a vertical plane, with an angular amplitude $\theta_0$. The tension in its string when it passes through the mean position is $2mg$. Neglecting the effect of air friction and the viscosity of air, the angular amplitude $\theta_0$ is

- (a) $30°$
- (b) $60°$
- (c) $90°$
- (d) $120°$
