# NSEP 2025 — Problema 15

If $P$ represents radiation pressure, $E$ represents radiation energy striking per unit area per unit time and $c$ represents speed of light then the possible values of non-zero integers $x$, $y$ and $z$ such that $P^x E^y c^z$ is dimensionless, may be

- (a) $x=1,\ y=-1,\ z=1$
- (b) $x=-1,\ y=1,\ z=1$
- (c) $x=1,\ y=1,\ z=-1$
- (d) $x=-1,\ y=-1,\ z=1$
