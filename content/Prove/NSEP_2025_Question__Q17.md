# NSEP 2025 — Problema 17

A pendulum consists of a heavy but very small bob of mass $M$ suspended at the end of a rigid rod of mass $m$ and length $L$. The time period of small oscillations in the vertical plane, about a horizontal axis through the upper end of the rod is

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 17**
![[NSEP_2025_Question_p6_f7.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) $2\pi\sqrt{\dfrac{m+2M}{m+3M}\cdot\dfrac{3L}{2g}}$
- (b) $2\pi\sqrt{\dfrac{m+3M}{m+2M}\cdot\dfrac{2L}{3g}}$
- (c) $2\pi\sqrt{\dfrac{3L}{2g}}$
- (d) $2\pi\sqrt{\dfrac{2m+M}{3m+M}\cdot\dfrac{3L}{2g}}$
