# NSEP 2025 — Problema 21

Three identical large metal plates are kept parallel and close to each other. Each plate can be treated as an ideal black body and has very high thermal conductivity. The first and third plates are maintained at high temperature $T_1 = 3T$ and $T_3 = 2T$. The temperature $T_2$ of the middle (i.e. second) plate under steady state condition is

- (a) $\dfrac{5T}{2}$
- (b) $\left(\dfrac{65}{2}\right)^{1/4}T$
- (c) $\left(\dfrac{97}{2}\right)^{1/4}T$
- (d) $\left(\dfrac{65}{4}\right)^{1/4}T$
