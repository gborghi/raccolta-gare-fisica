# NSEP 2025 — Problema 23

One mole of an ideal monoatomic gas, contained in a cylinder fitted with movable piston, is originally at $P_1$, $V_1$ and $T_1 = 27\ °\text{C}$. The gas is slowly heated. Initially 8.31 watt-hour of energy is added to it; at the same time it is allowed to expand at constant pressure to a new state $P_1$, $V_2$ and $T_2$. The correct option is

- (a) Value of $T_2$ is 1740 °C
- (b) Work done by the gas is 2160 $R$ joule
- (c) Internal energy of the gas increases by 1440 $R$ joule
- (d) $\dfrac{V_2}{V_1} = 5.8$
