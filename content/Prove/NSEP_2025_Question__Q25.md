# NSEP 2025 — Problema 25

Energy from the Sun falls on the Earth surface at the rate of $1400\ \text{W/m}^2$, which is known as solar constant. The respective rms values $E_\text{rms}$ and $B_\text{rms}$ of electric and magnetic fields in the sunlight (electromagnetic radiation) reaching Earth surface are (Take speed of light $c = 3\times10^8\ \text{ms}^{-1}$)

- (a) $E_\text{rms} = 726.5\ \text{V/m}$, $B_\text{rms} = 2.42\ \mu\text{T}$
- (b) $E_\text{rms} = 7260\ \text{V/m}$, $B_\text{rms} = 242\ \text{nT}$
- (c) $E_\text{rms} = 1030\ \text{V/m}$, $B_\text{rms} = 3.42\ \mu\text{T}$
- (d) $E_\text{rms} = 10300\ \text{V/m}$, $B_\text{rms} = 342\ \text{nT}$
