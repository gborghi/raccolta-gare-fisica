# NSEP 2025 — Problema 27

Two electric charges, $+q$ at the origin O $(0,0)$ and $-2q$ at the point A $(6,0)$ are placed on the $x$ axis. The locus of the point P in the $x$-$y$ plane where the potential vanishes ($V=0$) is

- (a) a straight line perpendicular to the $x$ axis and passing through $(2,0)$
- (b) only the point $(2,0)$
- (c) a circle with center at $(-2,0)$ and radius 4
- (d) an ellipse with foci at O and A
