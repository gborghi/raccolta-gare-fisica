# NSEP 2025 — Problema 30

Two infinitely long straight parallel wires perpendicular to the plane of the paper are 5 m apart. One of the wires, P, carries current $I$ out of the plane of the paper and the other, Q, carries the current $I$ into the plane of paper. The magnetic field $\vec{B}$ at the origin O of the coordinate system with $x$ and $y$ axes as perpendicular and parallel to PQ, respectively, is [Given $OP = 4\ \text{m}$ and $OQ = 3\ \text{m}$]

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 30**
![[NSEP_2025_Question_p8_f15.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) $\dfrac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi}\left(-\dfrac{3}{5}\hat{i} + \hat{j}\right)$
- (b) $\dfrac{\mu_0 I}{\pi}\left(\dfrac{7}{5}\hat{i} + \dfrac{24}{\pi}\hat{j}\right)$
- (c) $\dfrac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi}\left(\dfrac{3}{5}\hat{i} - \dfrac{8}{\pi}\hat{j}\right)$
- (d) $\dfrac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi}\left(-\dfrac{3}{24}\hat{i} + \hat{j}\right)$
