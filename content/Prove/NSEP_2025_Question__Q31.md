# NSEP 2025 — Problema 31

Charge $q$ is uniformly distributed over the surface of a thin non-conducting annular disc of inner radius $R_1$ and outer radius $R_2$. The disc is made to rotate with constant frequency $f$, about an axis passing through the center of the annular disc and perpendicular to its plane. The magnetic moment of the disc is

- (a) $\dfrac{\pi f q}{2}(R_2^2 + R_1^2)$
- (b) $\dfrac{\pi f q}{2}(R_2^2 - R_1^2)$
- (c) $\dfrac{\pi f q}{4}(R_2^2 - R_1^2)$
- (d) $2\pi f q(R_2^2 - R_1^2)$
