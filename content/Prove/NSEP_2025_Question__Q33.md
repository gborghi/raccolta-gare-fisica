# NSEP 2025 — Problema 33

The cutoff wavelength $\lambda_0$ (the maximum wavelength that can produce photoelectric effect) for the metal is

- (a) 500 nm
- (b) 550 nm
- (c) 600 nm
- (d) 750 nm
