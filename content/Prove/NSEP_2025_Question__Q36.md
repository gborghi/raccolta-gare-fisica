# NSEP 2025 — Problema 36

A certain substance, with a dielectric constant $k = 2.5$ and the dielectric strength $E = 1.8\times10^7\ \text{N/C}$, completely fills the space between the plates of a parallel plate capacitor (with circular plates) of capacitance $C = 72.0\ \text{nF}$. The minimum diameter of the circular plates, to ensure that the capacitor can withstand a potential difference of $V = 4.0\ \text{kV}$, is

- (a) 12 cm
- (b) 24 cm
- (c) 48 cm
- (d) 96 cm
