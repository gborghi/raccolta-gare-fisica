# NSEP 2025 — Problema 40

Imagine a situation, in which an infinite sheet with positive charge $+\sigma$ per unit area lies in the $xy$-plane and a second infinite sheet with negative charge $-\sigma$ per unit area lies in the $yz$-plane. The net electric field $\vec{E}$ at any point $(x, y, z)$ [that does not lie on either of these planes $xy$ or $yz$] can be expressed as

- (a) $\vec{E} = \dfrac{\sigma}{2\varepsilon_0}(\hat{i} + \hat{k})$
- (b) $\vec{E} = \dfrac{\sigma}{2\varepsilon_0}\hat{j}$
- (c) $\vec{E} = \dfrac{\sigma}{2\varepsilon_0}\left(\dfrac{x}{|x|}\hat{i} + \dfrac{z}{|z|}\hat{k}\right)$
- (d) $\vec{E} = \dfrac{\sigma}{\varepsilon_0}\left(\dfrac{x}{|x|}\hat{i} + \dfrac{z}{|z|}\hat{k}\right)$
