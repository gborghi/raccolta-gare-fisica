# NSEP 2025 — Problema 44

A thin uniform rod, of length $\ell = 0.200\ \text{m}$ with negligible mass, is attached to the floor by a frictionless hinge at a fixed point P. A horizontal spring connects the other end of the rod to a vertical wall. The rod is in a uniform magnetic field $B = 0.500\ \text{T}$ directed into the plane of paper. There is a current $i = 10.0\ \text{A}$ in the rod in the direction shown. Force constant of the spring is $5.00\ \text{N/m}$. The rod is in equilibrium at $\theta = \tan^{-1}\dfrac{4}{3}$.

Statement (1): Torque on the rod due to magnetic force is 0.1 Nm clockwise.

Statement (2): In equilibrium the energy stored in the spring is 0.039 J.
