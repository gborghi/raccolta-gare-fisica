# NSEP 2025 — Problema 55

One mole of an ideal monoatomic gas of molecular mass $M$ undergoes a cyclic process (ABCA) shown in the figure as a density $\rho$ versus pressure ($P$) curve. The correct option(s) is/are

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 55**
![[NSEP_2025_Question_p14_f28.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) Work done on the gas in going from A to B is $W_{AB} = \dfrac{MP_0}{\rho_0}\ln 2$.
- (b) Work done by the gas in the process BC is $W_{BC} = \dfrac{MP_0}{2\rho_0}$.
- (c) Efficiency ($\eta$) of the complete cycle ABCA is $\eta = \dfrac{2}{5}\left(1 - \dfrac{1}{\ln 2}\right)$.
- (d) Heat rejected by the gas in the complete cycle ABCA is $Q_{ABCA} = \dfrac{MP_0}{\rho_0}(1 - \ln 2)$.
