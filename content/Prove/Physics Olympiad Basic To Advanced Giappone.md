## Problema 1

**Problem 1.1. The SI and the cgs systems**

Express the following physical quantities in SI units (rationalized MKSA units).

(1) The unit of force in the cgs system is dyn (dyne). Express $1\,\text{dyn}$ in SI units (N).

(2) The unit of pressure in the cgs system is $\text{dyn/cm}^2$. Express $1\,\text{dyn/cm}^2$ in SI units (Pa).

(3) The unit of energy in the cgs system is erg. Express $1\,\text{erg}$ in SI units (J).

(4) The unit of viscosity in the cgs system is P (poise). Express $1\,\text{P}$ in SI units ($\text{Pa}\cdot\text{s}$).

(5) The unit of electric charge in the Gaussian CGS system is statcoulomb (statC). The Coulomb force between two point charges each with $1\,\text{statC}$ placed $1\,\text{cm}$ apart is $1\,\text{dyn}$. Express $1\,\text{statC}$ in SI units (C). Here, the proportional constant in Coulomb's law in SI units is $k_0 = 9.0 \times 10^9\,\text{N}\cdot\text{m}^2/\text{C}^2$.

(the 1st Challenge)
