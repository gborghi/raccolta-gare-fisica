## Problema 4







The Crocodile thought of four unit squares of a $2018 \times 2018$ grid forming a rectangle with sides $1$ and $4$. The Bear can choose any square formed by $9$ unit squares ($3 \times 3$) and ask whether it contains at least one of the four Crocodile's squares. What minimum number of questions should he ask to be sure of at least one affirmative answer?

## Problema 5







Find all real $a$ for which there exists a function $f : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ such that

$$f\!\left(x - f(y)\right) = f(x) + a\,\lfloor y \rfloor$$

for every real $x$, $y$, where $\lfloor y \rfloor$ denotes the integral part of $y$.

## Problema 6







A convex hexagon $ABCDEF$ is inscribed in a circle with radius $R$. Diagonals $AD$ and $BE$, $BE$ and $CF$, $AD$ and $CF$ of the hexagon meet at points $M$, $N$, $K$ respectively. Let $r_1, r_2, r_3, r_4, r_5, r_6$ be the inradii of the triangles $ABM$, $BCN$, $CDK$, $DEM$, $EFN$, $AFK$ respectively. Prove that

$$r_1 + r_2 + r_3 + r_4 + r_5 + r_6 \leq R\sqrt{3}.$$
