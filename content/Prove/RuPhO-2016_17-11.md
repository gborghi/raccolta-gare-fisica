behind the Earth is finite.
1. Let refraction (the phenomenon of refraction of the sun’s rays in the Earth’s atmosphere) be absent.
At what distance $L_1$ from the Earth will the total shadow still be observed? Find the duration of the
total lunar eclipse in this case.

atmosphere has a reduced height $h = 8\ \text{km}$ and an average refractive index $\eta = 1.00028$. Assuming
that the shadow boundary is formed by rays tangential to the Earth’s surface, determine at what maximal distance $L_2$ a total shadow will be observed now. What part of the lunar disk area will be darkened?
