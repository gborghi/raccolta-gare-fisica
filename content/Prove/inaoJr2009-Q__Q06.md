# INAO Junior 2009 — Problema 6

A repairman on the T.V. tower finds his water bottle leaking at the rate of 5 ml per second. He drops the bottle and it reaches the ground straight. If he was at a height of 125 m at that time and there was 200 ml of water left in the bottle, the amount of water left in the bottle (neglecting air resistance) just before it hit the ground was [3]

- (a) 175 ml
- (b) 50 ml
- (c) 100 ml
- (d) 200 ml
