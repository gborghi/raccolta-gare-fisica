# INAO Junior 2009 — Problema 10

Two glass tubes filled with water are held vertical and connected by a plastic tube as shown in the figure. Pans are mounted on top of each piston such that

$$(\text{weight of the piston} + \text{pan})_A = (\text{weight of the piston} + \text{pan})_B$$

radius of the piston $A = 1.0$ cm and radius of piston $B = 1.5$ cm. A 30.0 gm of mass is added in pan B, what is the mass required in pan A to balance 30.0 gm in pan B? [3]
