# INAO Junior 2009 — Problema 13

There is a regular bus service between Pune and Mumbai (180 km apart) at every hour from both the ends, from 4am to 11pm. These buses run at average speed of 45 km/hr. Taxies also run on the same route at 60 km/hr with regular interval of 30 min from 5am to 10pm. Following statements are based upon the number of taxies or buses crossed (not overtaken) only during travelling i.e. excluding instances of arrival and departure. Select the correct statement(s). [5]

- (a) First ST bus crosses 6 taxies.
- (b) Last taxi crosses 5 buses.
- (c) Bus left at 8pm crosses 10 taxies.
- (d) Taxi left at 12 noon crosses 6 buses.
