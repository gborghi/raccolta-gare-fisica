# INAO Junior 2010 — Problema 15

**Section C (analytical questions — 5 questions totalling 50 marks).**

An alien civilisation on a star far far away came to know about the Astronomy Olympiad Examination and wanted to test the smartness of the students. They sent the following two coded secret messages. Decode them.

(a) First is a pictorial message in black and white colour. They sent it on radio waves in the form of ones and zeros. Find out what the picture says. [5]

```
00000 00000 00000 00000 00000 00000 11111 01100 00100 00100 00011
10000 01000 10100 01000 10100 01101 10000 10001 00100 10001 11000
10001 00001 00010 00101 00100 01001 10110 01111 10100 00110 10000
01001 11000 00000 00000 00000 00000 00000 000
```

(b) Surprisingly, the aliens are also proficient in English and the following coded message is actually a sentence in the English language. In the answersheet, write down the coded sentence and also the true meaning of each alphabet in the code. [5]

> "Up tpmwf uif qsfwjpvt tvcrvftujpo uijol pg uif nfttbhf tfou cz uif bsfdjcp ufmftdpqf up bmjfot."
