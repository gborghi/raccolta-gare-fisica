# INAO Junior 2010 — Problema 17

Study the following image of the night sky (bigger version is printed in your answer-sheet) for mid-night on a certain day at a certain place and answer the questions below it. All answers must be marked / written on the answer-sheet.

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 17**
![[inaoJr2010-Q_p6_f1.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

(a) Mark all the four directions on the map. [2]

(b) Is this place in northern part of India or the southern part? [1]

(c) Mark and name 2 constellations each from the following two lists: [3]

- 0.5 marks each; any 2: Orion, Ursa Major, Taurus, Leo, Cygnus, Scorpio
- 1 mark each; any 2: Hydra, Corvus, Aquarius, Cancer, Canis Major, Aries

(d) Sketch the rough band of ecliptic i.e. apparent path of the Sun, the Moon and all the planets in the sky. [2]

(e) In which month the sky will appear like this at this time? Give reason in one line. [2]
