(a) In the figure below, a tank of water (height of water column $b$) is kept on an electronic weighing scale. A metal cube (side $a$ and density less than that of water) is hung from a spring balance and the spring balance is slowly lowered into the tank until it touches the bottom of the tank. The distance between the top of the tank and the bottom of the cube is denoted by $h$.
