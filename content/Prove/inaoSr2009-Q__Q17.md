# INAO Senior 2009 — Problema 17

**(γ)** The famous Indian astronomer, Aryabhata, expressed the value of $\pi$ in what we now know as continuing fractions, i.e.

$$\pi = 3.1416 = a + \cfrac{1}{b + \cfrac{1}{c + \cfrac{1}{d}}}$$

where $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$ are positive integers. Find $a$, $b$, $c$, $d$. [8]
