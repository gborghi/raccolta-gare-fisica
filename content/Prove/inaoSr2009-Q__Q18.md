# INAO Senior 2009 — Problema 18

**(δ)** Kedar sent a container of marbles by road from Mumbai to Parag in Pune. The container was $2\text{ m} \times 2\text{ m} \times 2.5\text{ m}$ in size, with height being the larger dimension. Marbles of 2 cm diameter were arranged to fill the entire base and then additional layers of marbles were arranged with each upper marble exactly on top of corresponding marble in the previous layer (see figure). The marbles were thus placed upto 2 m height to complete the cubical structure. However, on reaching Pune, when Parag opened the container, he found the height of structure was not 2 m as promised by Kedar, but something else. Kedar defended saying that marbles may have readjusted due to jiggling. Can you find new height of this marble pile? [10]
