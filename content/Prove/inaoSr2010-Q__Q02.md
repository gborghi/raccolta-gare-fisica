# INAO Senior 2010 — Problema 2

A wooden cube of length 5 units is painted on all faces and then cut into 85 smaller cubes of varying sizes. The sides of each of the smaller cubes are some integer number of units. All bigger cubes are carved out of the corners of the original cube. Answer the following three questions.

How many cubes have no side painted? [3]

- (a) 15
- (b) 17
- (c) 20
- (d) 27
