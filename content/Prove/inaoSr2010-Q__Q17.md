# INAO Senior 2010 — Problema 17

Study the following image of the night sky (a bigger version is printed in your answersheet) for mid-night on a certain day at a certain place and answer the questions below it. All answers must be marked / written on the answer-sheet.

**Quesito 17**
![[inaoSr2010-Q_p6_f1.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

(a) [2] Mark all the four directions on the map.

(b) [1] Is this place in the northern part of India or the southern part?

(c) [3] Mark and name 2 constellations each from the following two lists:
- 0.5 marks each; any 2: Orion, Ursa Major, Taurus, Leo, Cygnus, Scorpio
- 1 mark each; any 2: Hydra, Corvus, Aquarius, Cancer, Canis Major, Aries

(d) [2] Sketch a rough band of the ecliptic i.e. apparent path of the Sun, the Moon and all the planets in the sky.

(e) [2] In which month will the sky appear like this at this time? Give the reason in one line.
