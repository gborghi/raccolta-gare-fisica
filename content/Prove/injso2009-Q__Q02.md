# INJSO 2009 — Problema 2

Suppose you are given three resistances of values $2$, $4$, $6$ ohm. Which one of the following values is not possible to get by arranging resistances in various combinations?

- (a) Less than $2$
- (b) Equal to $4.4$
- (c) Equal to $7.33$
- (d) Equal to $6.75$
