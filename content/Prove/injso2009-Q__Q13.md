# INJSO 2009 — Problema 13

A satellite orbits the Earth in a circle of radius $8000$ km. At that distance from the Earth, $g = 6.2\ \mathrm{m/s^2}$. The velocity of the satellite is

- (a) $0.90$ km/s
- (b) $8.9$ km/s
- (c) $7.0$ km/s
- (d) impossible without knowing the satellite's mass
