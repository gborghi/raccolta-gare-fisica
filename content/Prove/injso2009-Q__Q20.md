# INJSO 2009 — Problema 20

A fixed mass of an ideal gas has a volume of $800\ \mathrm{cm^3}$ under certain conditions. The pressure (in kPa) and temperature (in K) are both doubled. What is the volume of the gas after these changes when other conditions remaining the same?

- (a) $200\ \mathrm{cm^3}$
- (b) $800\ \mathrm{cm^3}$
- (c) $1600\ \mathrm{cm^3}$
- (d) $3200\ \mathrm{cm^3}$
