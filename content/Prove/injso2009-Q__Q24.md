# INJSO 2009 — Problema 24

Using the equations below:

$$\mathrm{C(s) + O_2(g) \to CO_2(g)} \qquad \Delta H = -390\ \mathrm{kJ}$$
$$\mathrm{Mn(s) + O_2(g) \to MnO_2(s)} \qquad \Delta H = -520\ \mathrm{kJ}$$

what is $\Delta H$ (in kJ) for the following reaction $\mathrm{MnO_2(s) + C(s) \to Mn(s) + CO_2(g)}$?

- (a) $910$
- (b) $130$
- (c) $-130$
- (d) $-910$
