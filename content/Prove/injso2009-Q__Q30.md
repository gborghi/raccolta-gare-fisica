# INJSO 2009 — Problema 30

The pH of solution $\mathbf{X}$ is $1$ and that of $\mathbf{Y}$ is $2$. Which statement is correct about the $\mathrm{H^+}$ ion concentrations in the two solutions?

- (a) $[\mathrm{H^+}]$ in $\mathbf{X}$ is half that in $\mathbf{Y}$.
- (b) $[\mathrm{H^+}]$ in $\mathbf{X}$ is twice that in $\mathbf{Y}$.
- (c) $[\mathrm{H^+}]$ in $\mathbf{X}$ is one tenth that in $\mathbf{Y}$.
- (d) $[\mathrm{H^+}]$ in $\mathbf{X}$ is ten times that in $\mathbf{Y}$.
