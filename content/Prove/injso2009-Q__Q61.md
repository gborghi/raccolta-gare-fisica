# INJSO 2009 — Problema 61

**Section B (long questions).** Section B: Questions 61 to 68 are 5 marks each. Marks will also be indicated in the questions if there are more than one sub to it.

(a) A tortoise is crawling Eastward with velocity $\sqrt3$ km/hr and a lazy rabbit is travelling southward with velocity $1$ km/hr. With the help of a diagram, find the magnitude and direction of the velocity of the tortoise as observed by the rabbit. Show your working clearly. ($2$ marks)

(b) A force $F$ varies with time according to $F = 4 + 12t$, where $F$ is in Newton and $t$ is in seconds. The force acts on a block of mass $m = 2.00$ kg, which is initially at rest on a frictionless horizontal surface. $F$ makes an angle of $30°$ with the horizontal. What will be the velocity of the body at the end of the $2^{\text{nd}}$ second i.e. the value of the velocity of the body at that instant? ($3$ marks)
