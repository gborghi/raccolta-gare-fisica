# INJSO 2010 — Problema 14

Given $1\,\mathrm{dm^3}$ of $0.15$ M HCl and $1\,\mathrm{dm^3}$ of $0.40$ M HCl. What is the maximum volume of $0.25$ M HCl which can be made by directly mixing the two solutions without adding water?

- (a) $1\,\mathrm{dm^3}$
- (b) $2\,\mathrm{dm^3}$
- (c) $0.667\,\mathrm{dm^3}$
- (d) $1.667\,\mathrm{dm^3}$
