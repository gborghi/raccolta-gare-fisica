# INJSO 2010 — Problema 25

A solution containing $\mathrm{Na_2CO_3}$ + NaOH requires 300 ml of 0.1N HCl using phenolphthalein indicator. Methyl orange is then added to the above titrated solution and a further 25 ml of 0.2 N HCl is required. What is the amount of NaOH present in the solution?

- (a) 0.8 g
- (b) 1.0 g
- (c) 1.5 g
- (d) 2.0 g
