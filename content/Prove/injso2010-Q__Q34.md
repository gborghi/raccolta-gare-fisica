# INJSO 2010 — Problema 34

The alimentary canal of insects consists of mouth, pharynx, oesophagus, crop, proventriculus, gizzard and hindgut. In insects and birds, the gizzard helps in grinding the food. Select the statements that most accurately compare the gizzards of the bird and of insect.

i. The gizzard of the bird contains gravel and the gizzard of insect have chitinous teeth.
ii. The gizzard of the bird is muscular but that of the insect is devoid of muscles.
iii. The gizzard in both cases is muscular.
iv. The gizzard of bird bears bristles which are absent in the gizzard of insect.

- (a) i and iii
- (b) i and ii
- (c) ii and iv
- (d) iii and iv
