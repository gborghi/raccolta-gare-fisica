# INJSO 2010 — Problema 55

A ray of light is incident on the midpoint of a glass prism surface at an angle of 25.0° with the normal. For the glass, $n = 1.55$, and the angle of prism is 30.0°. What is the angle of incidence at the glass-to-air surface on the side opposite where the ray entered the prism?

- (a) 14.2°
- (b) 9.8°
- (c) 19.2°
- (d) light will not come out from the other surface
