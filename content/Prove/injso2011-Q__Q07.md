# INJSO 2011 — Problema 7

When unequal number of unpaired electrons are aligned in opposite direction, then the net magnetic moment is not zero. Such substances are termed as- [1]

- (a) diamagnetic
- (b) ferromagnetic
- (c) ferrimagnetic
- (d) antiferromagnetic
