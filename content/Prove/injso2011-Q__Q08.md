# INJSO 2011 — Problema 8

A man of height $h$ walking away from a lamp post finds his shadow to be equal to his height when he is at a distance $x$ from the lamp post. If the height of the lamp post is $H$, then $x$ is [1]

- (a) $H + h$
- (b) $H - h$
- (c) $H \cdot h$
- (d) $H / h$
