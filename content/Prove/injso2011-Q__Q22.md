# INJSO 2011 — Problema 22

A block of mass 5 kg is to be dragged along a rough horizontal surface having $\mu_s = 0.5$ and $\mu_k = 0.3$. The horizontal force applied for dragging it is 20 N. Acceleration of the block in $\text{m/s}^2$ and frictional force acting on the block in N are respectively [1]

- (a) 0, 20
- (b) 30, 15
- (c) 30, 25
- (d) 0, 15
