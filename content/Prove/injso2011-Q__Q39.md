# INJSO 2011 — Problema 39

In the reaction:

i) $\text{BaO}_2\text{(s)} + \text{O}_2\text{(g)} \longrightarrow \text{BaO (s)} + 2\text{O}_2\text{(g)}$
ii) $\text{ZnS (s)} + \text{4O}_2\text{(g)} \longrightarrow \text{ZnSO}_4\text{(s)} + 4\text{O}_2\text{(g)}$ [1]

- (a) $\text{O}_2$ is reduced both in i) & ii)
- (b) $\text{O}_2$ is oxidized both in i) & ii)
- (c) $\text{O}_2$ is oxidized in i) & reduced in ii)
- (d) $\text{O}_2$ is reduced in i) & oxidized in ii)
