# INJSO 2011 — Problema 54

Two solid cylinders 1 and 2 (mass $M_1 > M_2$ and radius $R_1 > R_2$) roll down from the rest on an inclined plane such that there is no loss of energy due to friction. Which of the spheres will reach at the bottom first [1]

- (a) cylinder 1
- (b) cylinder 2
- (c) cylinder with greater moment of inertia
- (d) both will reach at same time
