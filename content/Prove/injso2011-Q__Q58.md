# INJSO 2011 — Problema 58

- (a) $[\text{H}^+]$ in X is half that in Y.
- (b) $[\text{H}^+]$ in X is twice that in Y.
- (c) $[\text{H}^+]$ in X is ten times of that in Y.
- (d) $[\text{H}^+]$ in X is hundred times that in Y.
