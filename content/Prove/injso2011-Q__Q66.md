# INJSO 2011 — Problema 66

a) Prove that a square of a natural number leaves either 0 or 1 as a remainder upon division by 4. (3 marks)

b) Find, with proof, all positive integers $n$ such that $n! + 2$ is the square of a natural number. (5 marks)
