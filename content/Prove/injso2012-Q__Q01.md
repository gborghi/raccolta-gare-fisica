# INJSO 2012 — Problema 1

**Section A (multiple choice).** Questions 1 to 60 are multiple choice with every correct answer carrying 1 mark and every wrong answer carrying $-0.25$ mark. [1]

A load is to be moved using a wheelbarrow. The total mass of the load and wheelbarrow is 60 kg. The magnitude of gravitational acceleration is $10\ \text{m s}^{-2}$.

What is the work done if the handle is raised by 50 cm?
