# INJSO 2012 — Problema 2

The electrons, identified by quantum numbers $n$ and $l$ can be placed in order of increasing energy, from the lowest to highest as

- i. $n = 4$ & $l = 1$
- ii. $n = 4$ & $l = 0$
- iii. $n = 3$ & $l = 2$
- iv. $n = 3$ & $l = 1$

- (a) iv < ii < iii < i
- (b) ii < iv < i < iii
- (c) i < iii < ii < iv
- (d) iii < i < iv < ii
