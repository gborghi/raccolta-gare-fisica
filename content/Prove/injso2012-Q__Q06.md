# INJSO 2012 — Problema 6

Twenty five micrograms ($25\ \mu\text{g}$) of DNA amounting five micromole ($5\ \mu\text{mole}$) was cut using a specific restriction enzyme into two pieces. Subsequently, when it was analyzed on an agarose gel it showed two bands of 200 bp and 800 bp. If the length of the original DNA was 1000 bp, which of the following options best quantifies each band?
