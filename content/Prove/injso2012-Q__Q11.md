# INJSO 2012 — Problema 11

Sumit evacuated a cylinder and filled in 4 g of gas A at temperature $25\ ^\circ\text{C}$. Pressure was found to be 1 atm. His friend Vineet, filled another 8 g of gas B in the cylinder at the same temperature. The final pressure was found to be 1.5 atm. The ratio of molecular masses of A and B (assuming ideal gas behaviour) is

- (a) 1 : 1
- (b) 1 : 2
- (c) 1 : 3
- (d) 1 : 4
