# INJSO 2012 — Problema 26

The equilibrium constant for the reaction

$$\text{N}_2(g) + \text{O}_2(g) \Leftrightarrow 2\text{NO}(g)$$

is $4.0 \times 10^{4}$ at 2000 K. In presence of a catalyst equilibrium is attained 10 times faster. Therefore equilibrium constant in presence of catalyst at 2000 K is

- (a) $40 \times 10^{4}$
- (b) $4 \times 10^{4}$
- (c) $4 \times 10^{3}$
- (d) none of these
