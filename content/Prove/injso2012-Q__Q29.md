# INJSO 2012 — Problema 29

Real gases can be described by the Van der Waal's equation as:

$$(P + a/V^2)(V - b) = nRT.$$

The compressibility factor for a gas is defined by $Z = pV/RT$. Which of the following statements is correct?

- (a) When $Z > 1$, real gases are difficult to compress than the ideal gases.
- (b) When $Z > 1$, real gases are easy to compress than the ideal gases.
- (c) When $Z = 1$, real gases are difficult to compress than the ideal gases.
- (d) When $Z < 1$, real gases are difficult to compress than the ideal gases.
