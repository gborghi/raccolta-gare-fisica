# INJSO 2012 — Problema 43

10 g of ice at $-10\ ^\circ\text{C}$ is added to 10 g of water at $85\ ^\circ\text{C}$. What is the final temperature and amount of ice left in the system? (System is kept inside an ideal insulator).

- (a) $0\ ^\circ\text{C}$, 0 g
- (b) $0\ ^\circ\text{C}$, 2 g
- (c) $37.5\ ^\circ\text{C}$, 0 g
- (d) $37.5\ ^\circ\text{C}$, 5 g
