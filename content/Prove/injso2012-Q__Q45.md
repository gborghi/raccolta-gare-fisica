# INJSO 2012 — Problema 45

The diagram shows a lift system in which the elevator (mass $m_1$) is partly counterbalanced by a heavy weight (mass $m_2$).

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 45**
![[injso2012-Q_p14_f1.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

At what rate does the motor provide energy to the system when the elevator is rising at a steady speed $v$ ? ($g$ = acceleration of free fall) (consider the pulley as frictionless at the pivot)

- (a) $(m_1 + m_2)gv$
- (b) $\tfrac{1}{2}(m_1 + m_2)gv$
- (c) $(m_1 g - m_2 g)v$, i.e. $(m_1 - m_2)gv$
- (d) $(m_1 - m_2)gv$
