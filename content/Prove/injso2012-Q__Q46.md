# INJSO 2012 — Problema 46

The following reaction is at equilibrium in a given cylinder of constant volume.

$$\text{N}_2(g) + 3\text{H}_2(g) \Leftrightarrow 2\text{NH}_3(g)$$

Addition of argon gas to the cylinder will

- (a) reduce the formation of NH$_3$ with no change in temperature of the equilibrium system.
- (b) Increase the formation of NH$_3$ with increase in temperature.
- (c) reduce the formation of NH$_3$ with decrease in temperature.
- (d) increase the formation of NH$_3$ with no change in temperature of the equilibrium system.
