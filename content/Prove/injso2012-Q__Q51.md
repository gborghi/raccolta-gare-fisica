# INJSO 2012 — Problema 51

A mixture of 0.3 moles of H$_2$ and 0.3 moles of I$_2$ are allowed to react in 10 litre evacuated flask at $500\ ^\circ\text{C}$. The reaction is

$$\text{H}_2 + \text{I}_2 \Leftrightarrow 2\text{HI}$$

Value of $k$ is found to be 64. The amount of I$_2$ present at equilibrium is

- (a) 0.15 mole
- (b) 0.06 mole
- (c) 0.03 mole
- (d) 0.2 mole
