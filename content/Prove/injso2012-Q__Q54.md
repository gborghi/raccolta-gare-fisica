# INJSO 2012 — Problema 54

If at the top of a mountain the temperature is $0\ ^\circ\text{C}$ and the pressure is 0.934 atm and at the bottom of the mountain, the temperature is $20\ ^\circ\text{C}$ and the pressure is 1 atm. The ratio of density (considering the ideal gas situation) of air at the top, to that at the bottom of the mountain is:

- (a) 1 : 1
- (b) 1 : 1.4
- (c) 1.04 : 1
- (d) 1.5 : 1
