# INJSO 2012 — Problema 62

(a) Esha exists at $-20\ ^\circ\text{C}$ up to height $h = 10$ cm in a uniform cylindrical vessel (with no air gaps). Water at temperature $+3\ ^\circ\text{C}$ is filled in another identical vessel up to the same height $h = 10$ cm. Now, water from second vessel is poured into first vessel and is found that level of upper surface falls through $h = 0.5$ cm when thermal equilibrium is reached.

Neglecting the thermal capacity of vessels, change in density of water due to change in temperature and loss of heat due to radiation, calculate the initial temperature $x$ $^\circ\text{C}$ of water. Use density of ice as 0.9 g/cc.

**Total: 2 Marks**

(b) A ball of mass 10 kg moving at 50 ms$^{-1}$ in N-E direction is forced to move at 30 ms$^{-1}$ in S-E direction in 10 sec by an application of a constant force. Find the force vector (magnitude and angle with respect to east).

**Total: 3 Marks**

**Total: 5 Marks**
