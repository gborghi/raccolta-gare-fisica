# INJSO 2013 — Problema 5

Respiratory quotient (R.Q.) is defined as the ratio of volume of $\text{CO}_2$ evolved to the volume of $\text{O}_2$ taken in during the respiration process. Value of R.Q. depends on the nature of respiratory substrate and to the extent to which this substance is broken down into simpler products. Which of the following situation will give us the R.Q. value as infinity?

- (a) Fats used as substrate under aerobic conditions.
- (b) Organic acid is used as a substrate under aerobic conditions.
- (c) Any type of substrate used under anaerobic conditions.
- (d) Any type of substrate used under aerobic conditions.
