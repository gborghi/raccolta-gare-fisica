# INJSO 2013 — Problema 29

1.84 g of Dolomite ($\text{CaMg(CO}_3)_2$) ore was heated resulting in a residue of constant weight 0.96 g. During heating the metal of one of the products burnt with a brick red flame and the second burnt with a dazzling white flame. The approximate percentage composition of the two products in the residue are respectively

- (a) 54 and 46
- (b) 46 and 54
- (c) 42 and 58
- (d) 58 and 42
