# INJSO 2013 — Problema 30

Colloid consists of dispersed phase and dispersion medium. Aerosol is one type of colloid. Aerosol is made up of which of the following combination?

I. Gas in liquid.
II. Liquid in gas.
III. Solid in gas.

- (a) II only.
- (b) I, II and III.
- (c) I and II only.
- (d) II and III only.
