# INJSO 2013 — Problema 31

Tyndall effect can be observed in a colloidal solution. Consider light scattering in the following:

I. When sunlight passes through the canopy of a dense forest.
II. When normal light passes through lead iodide solution.
III. When monochromatic light passes through solution of $\text{K}_2\text{SO}_4(\text{Al})_2\text{SO}_4 \cdot 24\text{H}_2\text{O}$.

Tyndall effect is observed in:

- (a) I, II and III.
- (b) I only.
- (c) I and III only.
- (d) III only.
