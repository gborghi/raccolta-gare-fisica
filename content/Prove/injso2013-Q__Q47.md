# INJSO 2013 — Problema 47

For a redox reaction between one mole of potassium dicromate and $x$ moles of hydrochloric acid, products are formed out of which one product is $y$ moles of chromyl chloride ($\text{CrCl}_3$). The values of $x$ and $y$ are:

- (a) 8 and 4 respectively.
- (b) 8 and 2 respectively.
- (c) 2 and 8 respectively.
- (d) 4 and 8 respectively.
