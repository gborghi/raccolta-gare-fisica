# INJSO 2013 — Problema 53

A 2.00 kg ball and a 1.00 kg ball collide with each other. The data from their collision is shown on the table given below:

| Time | 2.00 kg ball $p_x$ | 2.00 kg ball $p_y$ | 1.00 kg ball $p_x$ | 1.00 kg ball $p_y$ |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Before collision | 15.00 | 0.00 | 0.00 | 5.00 |
| After collision | 12.00 | 9.00 | 3.00 | -4.00 |

Here $p_x$ and $p_y$ are $x$ and $y$ components respectively of linear momentum. The angle between the balls after collision is:

- (a) $150°$
- (b) $120°$
- (c) $90°$
- (d) $60°$
