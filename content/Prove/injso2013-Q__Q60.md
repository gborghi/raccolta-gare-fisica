# INJSO 2013 — Problema 60

A 100 m sprinter increases her speed from rest uniformly at the rate of 1 m/s² upto 40 m and covers the remaining distance with uniform speed. The sprinter covers the first half of the run in $t_1$ s and the second half in $t_2$ s. Then:

- (a) $t_1 > t_2$
- (b) $t_1 < t_2$
- (c) $t_1 = t_2$
- (d) information given is incomplete.
