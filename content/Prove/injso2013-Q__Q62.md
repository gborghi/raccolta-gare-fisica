# INJSO 2013 — Problema 62

The atmospheric pressure on the earth's surface is $P_a$ N/m². A table of area 2 m² is tilted at $45°$ to the horizontal. The force on the top surface of the table due to the atmosphere is (in newtons):

- (a) $2 P_a$
- (b) $\sqrt{2} P_a$
- (c) $2\sqrt{2} P_a$
- (d) $P_a/\sqrt{2}$
