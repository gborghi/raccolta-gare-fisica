# INJSO 2013 — Problema 75

The absolute refractive index of medium $P$ is 1.5. When light is incident on an interface between medium $P$ and medium $Q$ at an angle of incidence of $30°$ in medium $P$, the angle of refraction is $\theta$ where $\sin\theta = 7/20$. The speed of light in medium $Q$ is given by

- (a) $1.4 \times 10^8$ m/s
- (b) $3.0 \times 10^8$ m/s
- (c) $2.5 \times 10^8$ m/s
- (d) $2.1 \times 10^8$ m/s
