# INJSO 2014 — Problema 18

For several days, the temperature at the bottom of a pond is $4\ ^\circ\text{C}$ and the air temperature just above the top surface of the pond is $-1\ ^\circ\text{C}$. Depth of the pond is L. The thermal conductivity of ice is 3 times that of water. Thickness of frozen layer of the ice formed must be

- (a) 3L/4
- (b) 4L/5
- (c) 3L/5
- (d) 3L/7
