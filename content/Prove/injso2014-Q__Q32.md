# INJSO 2014 — Problema 32

A white crystalline salt P reacts with dilute HCl to liberate a suffocating gas Q and also forms a yellow precipitate. The gas Q turns potassium dichromate acidified with $\text{H}_2\text{SO}_4$ to a green coloured solution R. P, Q and R are?

- (a) P: $\text{Na}_2\text{S}_2\text{O}_3$, Q: $\text{SO}_2$, R: $\text{Cr}_2(\text{SO}_4)_3$
- (b) P: $\text{Na}_2\text{SO}_4$, Q: $\text{Cl}_2$, R: $\text{Cr}(\text{SO}_4)_3$
- (c) P: $\text{Na}_2\text{SO}_4$, Q: $\text{SO}_2$, R: $\text{Cr}_2(\text{SO}_4)_3$
- (d) P: $\text{Na}_2\text{S}$, Q: $\text{Cl}_2$, R: $\text{Cr}_2(\text{SO}_4)_3$
