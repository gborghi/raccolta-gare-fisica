# INJSO 2014 — Problema 42

Child drinks a liquid of density d through a vertical straw. Atmospheric pressure is $P_0$ and the child is capable of lowering the pressure at the top of the straw by 10%. The acceleration of the free fall is g. What is the maximum length of straw that would enable the child to drink the liquid?

- (a) $P_0/10\ \rho g$
- (b) $9P_0/10\ \rho g$
- (c) $P_0/\rho g$
- (d) $10P_0/\rho g$
