# INJSO 2014 — Problema 65

A particle is moving on the real line, and its position is observed at four different time stamps. At time $t = 0$, the particle is at $x = 0$, at time $t = 20$ seconds, we have $x = 40$, at time $t = 40$ seconds, $x = 60$ and at time $t = 60$ seconds, we have $x = 90$. Show that at some point of time between 0 and 60 seconds, the acceleration of the particle was zero. **[5 Marks]**
