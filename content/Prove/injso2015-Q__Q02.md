# INJSO 2015 — Problema 2

Units of length, velocity and force in a certain system of units are double of those in SI (i.e. unit length in this system is 2 m, and so on for force and velocity). Read the following statements.

i) Unit of mass is unchanged.
ii) Unit of time is unchanged.
iii) Unit of linear momentum is doubled.
iv) Unit of energy is doubled.

Among the above statements

- (a) All four are correct.
- (b) Only i, ii and iii are correct.
- (c) Only i and ii are correct.
- (d) Only ii and iii are correct.
