# INJSO 2015 — Problema 7

Rajesh was performing the thermometric titration with caustic potash and oil of vitriol, under the same set of conditions. He dissolved 19.6 g of oil of vitriol in $1\ \text{dm}^3$ of water and 5.6 g of caustic potash in $1\ \text{dm}^3$ of water. Find the volume of oil of vitriol taken initially for thermometric titration. The observation table of his experiment is as follows.

| Obs No. | Volume of KOH added / $\text{cm}^3$ | Temperature $^\circ\text{C}$ |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 20 | 27 |
| 2 | 30 | 27.4 |
| 3 | 40 | 27.5 |
| 4 | 50 | 27.8 |
| 5 | 60 | 27.6 |
| 6 | 70 | 27.2 |
| 7 | 80 | 26.9 |

- (a) $6.25\ \text{cm}^3$
- (b) $50\ \text{cm}^3$
- (c) $12.5\ \text{cm}^3$
- (d) $25\ \text{cm}^3$
