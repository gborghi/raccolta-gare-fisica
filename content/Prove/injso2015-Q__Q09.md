# INJSO 2015 — Problema 9

Air of density $\rho$, moving with velocity $v$ strikes normally on an inclined surface (having area A) of a wedge of mass m kept on a horizontal surface. Collisions are perfectly elastic (No loss of kinetic energy). Minimum coefficient of static friction between wedge and the horizontal surface, for the wedge to be stationary, is

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 9**
![[injso2015-Q_p4_f1.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) $\dfrac{\rho A v^2}{mg + \rho A v^2 \cos\theta}$
- (b) $\dfrac{2\rho A v^2 \sin\theta}{mg + 2\rho A v^2 \cos\theta}$
- (c) $\dfrac{\rho A v^2 \tan\theta}{mg}$
- (d) $\tan\theta$
