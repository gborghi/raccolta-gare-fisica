# INJSO 2015 — Problema 15

A mass hanging with a spring suspended from a ceiling is pulled down and released. The mass then oscillates with simple harmonic motion of period T. The graph shows how its distance from the ceiling varies with time. What can be deduced from the graph?

<!--fig:start-->
**Quesito 15**
![[injso2015-Q_p6_f1.png]]
<!--fig:end-->

- (a) The amplitude of oscillations is 70 cm.
- (b) The kinetic energy is maximum at $t = T/2$.
- (c) The speed is maximum at $t = T/4$.
- (d) The restoring force on the mass increases between $t = 0$ and $t = T/4$.
