# INJSO 2015 — Problema 20

Soaps (sodium salt of fatty acid) are the molecules in which the two ends have differing properties, one is hydrophilic whereas other is hydrophobic. Hydrophobic part refers to tail of the soap which is always out of water.

Which of the following statement is true about soap?

| | Hydrophilic | Hydrophobic |
|---|---|---|
| (a) | Sodium | Fatty acid |
| (b) | Fatty acid | Sodium |
| (c) | Glycerine | Sodium |
| (d) | Sodium | Ester |
