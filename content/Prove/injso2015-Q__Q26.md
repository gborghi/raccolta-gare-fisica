# INJSO 2015 — Problema 26

A bottle of 200 ml of hydrogen peroxide was accidentally left open for long time. Which of the following facts hold good for the chemical reaction that takes place? ($\text{Kp}$ is an equilibrium constant in terms of partial pressures of reactants and products and $\text{Kc}$ is equilibrium constant in terms of molar concentrations of reactants and products).

- (a) $\text{Kp} = \text{Kc}$
- (b) $\text{Kp} > \text{Kc}$
- (c) $\text{Kp} < \text{Kc}$
- (d) $\text{Kp}$ and $\text{Kc}$ cannot be correlated
