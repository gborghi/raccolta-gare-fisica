# INJSO 2015 — Problema 35

(a) A cylindrical vessel of diameter 12 cm contains $736\ \text{cm}^3$ of water. A cylindrical solid glass piece of diameter 8 cm and height 8 cm is placed in the vessel. If a point object at the bottom of the vessel under the glass piece is seen by paraxial rays, looking at the image of this object and find the total apparent shift of the bottom. Refractive index of water $= 4/3$ and refractive index of glass $= 3/2$. **[3 marks]**

(b) The sharpest image of the sun cast by a converging lens with focal length 25 cm has a diameter of 0.5 cm. A diverging lens of focal length 10 cm is placed at 15 cm from the converging lens on the other side of the sun. Determine the size of the final image and its position with respect to the diverging lens? **[2 marks]**
