# INJSO 2015 — Problema 39

A mixture of $\text{H}_2\text{C}_2\text{O}_4$ (oxalic acid) and $\text{NaHC}_2\text{O}_4$ weighing 2.02 g was dissolved in water and this solution was made upto one liter. Two different solutions were carried out and the result obtained: 10 ml of the solution required 3 ml of 0.1NaOH for complete neutralization. Calculate the amount of $\text{H}_2\text{C}_2\text{O}_4$ and $\text{NaHC}_2\text{O}_4$ in the mixture? **[5 Marks]**
