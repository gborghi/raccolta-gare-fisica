# INJSO 2015 — Problema 42

Aruna was studying the interaction between various chemicals. She found that when she mixed potassium bromate ($\text{KBrO}_3$) with potassium bromide (KBr) and acidified the solution with $\text{H}_2\text{SO}_4$, vapours of $\text{Br}_2$ were evolved. On addition of KI to the reaction mixture, a deep brown colour was obtained due to formation of iodine, and this could be titrated with sodium thiosulphate ($\text{Na}_2\text{S}_2\text{O}_3$) using starch indicator.

  i. Write down the balanced reaction occurring at each stage. **[3 Marks]**
  ii. In what molar proportion should $\text{KBrO}_3$ and KBr be mixed? **[1 Mark]**
  iii. Starting with 10 ml of 0.01M $\text{KBrO}_3$ and using excess of KBr, $\text{H}_2\text{SO}_4$ & KI, what volume of 0.05 M $\text{Na}_2\text{S}_2\text{O}_3$ will be required? **[1 Mark]**
