# INJSO 2016 — Problema 9

Imagine a conductor in a cylindrical shape of radius *R*. Two thin circular discs of radius *R* made up of non-conducting material, carrying charge + *Q* and − *Q* respectively, are attached to the two ends of this cylinder. Consider following statements.

I. Free or conduction electrons in the conducting cylinder will drift towards +*Q*.
II. A constant current will be set up through cylinder.
III. Constant current will flow for very short duration of time.

- (a) Only I is true.
- (b) Only II is true.
- (c) Only III is true.
- (d) Only I and III are true.
