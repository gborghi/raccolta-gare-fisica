# INJSO 2016 — Problema 18

Two plane mirrors are kept with their reflecting surfaces inclined at 100°. A mosquito flying in between the combination of these mirror finds multiple images of itself. At a particular moment the mosquito is very close to one of the mirrors. How many images will be formed and how many images can the mosquito see?

- (a) 2, 1
- (b) 3, 1
- (c) 4, 2
- (d) 3, 2
