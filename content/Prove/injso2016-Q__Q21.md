# INJSO 2016 — Problema 21

Anand wanted to prepare a salt solution of pH 1.0. For that he used solutions of a strong monovalent base and acid. He tried different combinations to obtain the required solution. Can you help him to decide which one is the most correct combination of statements given below?

I. 100ml of (M/10) acid and 100ml of (M/10) alkali.
II. 55ml of (M/10) acid and 45ml of (M/10) alkali.
III. 10ml of (M/10) acid and 90ml of (M/10) alkali.
IV. 75ml of (M/5) acid and 25ml of (M/5) alkali.

- (a) I and III
- (b) II and IV
- (c) Only II
- (d) Only IV
