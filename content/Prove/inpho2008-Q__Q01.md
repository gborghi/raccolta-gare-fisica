# INPhO 2008 — Problema 1

We define three quantities as follow:

$$A = m_e c^2, \quad B = h/m_e c, \quad C = e^2/2\epsilon_0 ch$$

Where $m_e$ is electron mass and other symbols have their usual meanings. For the hydrogen atom, express the radius of the $n^\text{th}$ Bohr orbit $r_n$, the energy level $E_n$, and the Rydberg constant $R$ in terms of any two of $\{A, B, C\}$.
