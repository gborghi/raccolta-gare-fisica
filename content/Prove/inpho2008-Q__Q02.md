# INPhO 2008 — Problema 2

Consider a ball which is projected horizontally with speed $u$ from the edge of a cliff of height $H$ as shown in the Fig. (1). There is air resistance proportional to the velocity in both $x$ and $y$ direction i.e. the motion in the $x$ ($y$) direction has air resistance given by the $c\,v_x$ ($c\,v_y$) where $c$ is the proportionality constant and $v_x(v_y)$ is the velocity in the $x$ ($y$) directions. Take the downward direction to be negative. The acceleration due to gravity is $g$. Take the origin of the system to be at the bottom of the cliff as shown in Fig. (1).

(a) Obtain expression for $x(t)$ and $y(t)$.

(b) Obtain the expression for the equation of trajectory.
