# INPhO 2008 — Problema 5

Sunlight falls on the convex surface of the plano-convex lens of aperture 0.080 m. The radius of curvature of the convex surface of the lens is 0.100 m. The refractive indices of the material of the lens for extreme red and violet colours of sunlight are 1.600 and 1.700 respectively.

(a) Calculate the positions of the observed image of the Sun with violet and red center.

(b) Calculate the sizes of the observed image of the sun with violet and red center.
