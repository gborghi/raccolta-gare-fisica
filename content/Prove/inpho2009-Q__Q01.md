# INPhO 2009 — Problema 1

**PART - C**

One mole of gas undergoes a linear process as shown in Fig. (18).

```tikz
\begin{document}
\begin{tikzpicture}[scale=1.3]
  \draw[->] (0,0) -- (3.2,0) node[right] {$V$};
  \draw[->] (0,0) -- (0,3.2) node[above] {$P$};
  \node[below left] at (0,0) {$(0,0)$};
  \draw[thick] (0,2.6) -- (2.6,0);
  \draw[dashed] (0,2.6) -- (0,2.6);
  \node[left] at (0,2.6) {$P_0$};
  \node[below] at (2.6,0) {$V_0$};
\end{tikzpicture}
\end{document}
```

1. Express $P$ in terms of $\{V, V_0, P_0\}$. **[1]**

2. Assuming that the gas is ideal, obtain the expression for $T$ in terms of gas constant $R$ and $\{V, V_0, P_0\}$. **[1]**

3. Obtain the expression for volume change with temperature $(dV/dT)$ in terms of $\{R, V, V_0, P_0\}$. **[1]**

4. Let $T_{\max}$ be the maximum temperature in the process. Express $T_{\max}$ in terms of $\{V_0, P_0, R\}$. **[1]**

5. Sketch the $T-V$ diagram. ($T$ on $y$-axis and $V$ on $x$-axis.) **[1.5]**

6. Let $C_p/C_v = \gamma$, where $C_p$ ($C_v$) is specific heat at constant pressure (volume). Express heat capacity $C_v$ in terms of $R$ and $\gamma$. **[0.5]**

7. Using the first law of thermodynamics, obtain the expression for specific heat $C$ for the above linear process in terms of $\{R, \gamma, V_0, V\}$. **[3]**

8. Suppose mixture consists of half mole of mono atomic and half mole of diatomic gas. Obtain $\gamma$ for this mixture. **[0.5]**

9. For the mixture described in Part (8), obtain $C$ in terms of $\{R, V, V_0\}$. **[1]**

10. Plot $C/R$ (on $y$-axis) vs $V/V_0$ ($x$-axis). **[2.5]**

[Marks: $1 + 1 + 1 + 1 + 1.5 + 0.5 + 3 + 0.5 + 1 + 2.5 = 13$]
