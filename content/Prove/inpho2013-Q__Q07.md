It rises up and rests at an equilibrium height in the atmosphere, where its weight is exactly balanced by the upward buoyant force. We shall assume ideal gas law to hold for all processes and neglect the mass of the balloon. If the balloon is displaced vertically, it is often found to oscillate about the equilibrium position.
