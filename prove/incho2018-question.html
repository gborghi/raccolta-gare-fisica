22 marks Keto-Enol Tautomerism: Kinetics and Thermodynamics

Two compounds having the same molecular formula but different structural arrangement of atoms in molecules are known as structural isomers, e.g., C2H6O represents both ethyl alcohol and dimethyl ether. One form of structural isomerism is tautomerism, in which the same molecule dynamically interconverts between two or more structural forms.
